The small European country of Andorra , situated between France and Spain , has reported its first case of the coronavirus . 
The patient is a 20 year old Andorran man who had recently traveled to Milan , Italy , said government press officer Judit Pedros . 
Italy is at the heart of the European outbreak , and has recorded 1,694 cases , including 34 deaths . 
In the past two weeks , more than a dozen European countries have reported their first cases of the virus .
