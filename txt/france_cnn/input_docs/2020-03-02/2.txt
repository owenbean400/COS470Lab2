The novel coronavirus has been found in a third French department , with nine new cases reported in Morbihan , northwest France . 
In France , departments are administrative areas ; the country is broken down into large regions , then local authorities in departments , then arrondissements and cantons . 
The two other departments to have reported infections are Oise and La Haute Savoie . 
The nine infections reported late on Sunday bring France 's national total to 130 cases .
