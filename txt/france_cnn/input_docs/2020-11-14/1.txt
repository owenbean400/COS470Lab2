Covid 19 hospital and intensive care unit admissions in France decreased sharply on Friday , according to the country 's health agency . 
The French Health Agency reported that the number of patients in hospital for coronavirus only increased by 22 between Thursday and Friday . 
There was an increase of 736 hospital patients the day before . 
The ministry reported that only three additional ICU beds were taken up by coronavirus patients on Friday . 
The increase was 95 on Thursday , and has been averaging around 100 per day for several weeks . 
France entered its second lockdown two weeks ago on Thursday .
