In an address to the nation on Sunday evening , French President Emmanuel Macron applauded the country in the battle against coronavirus . 
He announced a further easing of restrictions that were put in place to battle the spread of Covid 19 . 
Macron said , " We can be proud of what has been done and of our country . " 
He said , " tens of thousands of lives have been saved by our choices , by our actions . " 
Macron said , starting Monday , mainland France will lift all restrictions previously applied to businesses and transport . 
" We must get our economy back on track whilst protecting the most vulnerable , " Macron said . 
Macron said France will follow the EU Commission recommendation of opening up borders on Monday .
