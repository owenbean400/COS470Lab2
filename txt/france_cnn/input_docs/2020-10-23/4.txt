French first lady Brigitte Macron has tested negative after a potential coronavirus exposure , the Elysée told CNN on Friday . 
She will resume her activities . 
On Monday , the French first lady was identified as a contact of someone who tested positive for coronavirus and she began self isolating . 
This did not change President Emmanuel Macron ’s schedule , the Elysée said . 
He was not in close contact with the person who later tested positive .
