Jamaica has confirmed its first case of coronavirus in the capital of Kingston , the Ministry of Health and Wellness announced Tuesday . 
The patient is a Jamaican woman that was in the UK recently but arrived back on the island on March 4 . 
She has been in isolation since Sunday . 
Jamaica is taking several steps to prevent community spread of the virus , including contact tracing . 
The country has imposed travel restrictions on eight countries : China , Italy , South Korea , Iran , Singapore , Spain , France and Germany .
