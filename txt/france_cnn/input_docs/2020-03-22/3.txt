The French Parliament adopted the state of emergency law Sunday night . 
The law gives a legal framework for the measures that have been put into place by the French government to mitigate the coronavirus crisis . 
The law was passed in an almost empty Parliament , adapted in order to respect health and security measures . 
The law passed with 510 votes in favor and 37 votes against , according to the Parliamentary report . 
On Thursday , French Prime Minister Edouard Philippe made a plea in favor of the state of emergency to the Senate , saying : " The government needs it . 
I need it . "
