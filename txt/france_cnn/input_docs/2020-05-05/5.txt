It 's still too soon for the French to consider their much cherished summer vacations , President Emmanuel Macron said Tuesday . 
Macron also cautioned citizens against planning international holidays . 
Macron added : " We are going to limit all international travel , even during summer vacation . 
We will stay among Europeans ... maybe as the epidemic evolves , we 'll even have to reduce that a little more , but it is too early to tell . 
We will know in early June . "
