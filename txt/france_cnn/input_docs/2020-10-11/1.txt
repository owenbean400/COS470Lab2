In France , 26,896 people tested positive for Covid 19 in the past 24 hours -- another record high for a daily increase in cases . 
The numbers were updated late Saturday on the French Health Authority website . 
On Friday , 20,339 new cases were reporting , breaking the 20,000 cases in a day threshold . 
The positivity rate ( number of people testing positive ) also keeps climbing . 
It now stands at 11 % , up from 7.6 % on October 1 and 4.3 % on September 1 . 
The number of people hospitalized in the ICU is also rising . 
As of Saturday night , 1,456 people were in intensive care , which is eight more than the day before , according to the French Health Authority website .
