The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention ( CDC ) has reported 5,119,711 cases of coronavirus . 
That is an increase of 55,540 cases from its previous count . 
The CDC said the number of deaths in the United States had risen by 1,244 to 163,651 . 
The CDC reported its tally of Covid 19 as of 4pm ET on 11 August versus its previous report a day earlier . 
The CDC figures do not necessarily reflect cases reported by individual states .
