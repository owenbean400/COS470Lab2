Mainland China reported 12 new Covid 19 cases on 9 December , down from 15 cases a day earlier , the national health authority said on Thursday . 
The National Health Commission said in a statement 11 of the new cases were imported infections originating from overseas . 
The commission said one locally transmitted case was reported in the Inner Mongolia region . 
The number of new asymptomatic cases , which China does not classify as confirmed cases , rose to five from one a day earlier . 
The total number of confirmed Covid 19 cases now stands at 86,673 , while the death toll remained unchanged at 4,634 .
