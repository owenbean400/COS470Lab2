South Korea ’s Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announced 231 new cases today , bringing the country ’s total to 833 in a massive jump . 161 cases were announced this morning , and another 70 in the afternoon . 
Among the 70 cases , 41 are from the city of Daegu , and 12 are from North Gyeongsang Province -- two places where the outbreak has been concentrated . 
The other cases are spread out across Seoul , Busan , Daejeon , Ulsan , and Gyeonggi Province . 
The total number of deaths related to the coronavirus is now at seven , with the latest death happening yesterday . 
About half of all cases nationwide are linked to a religious group in the south of the country .
