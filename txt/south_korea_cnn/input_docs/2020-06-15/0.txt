South Korea reported 37 new coronavirus cases on Sunday and no new deaths , according to the Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention . 
The total number of reported cases in the country stands at 12,121 and there are more than 1,100 patients in active quarantine . 
Of the 37 new cases , 24 are locally transmitted . 
Of those , 22 occurred in the capital Seoul Despite strengthened infection prevention measures , the average number of daily cases from the Seoul Metropolitan area increased from 20.4 cases during the period May 17 to May 30 , to 36.5 average cases during May 31 to June 13 .
