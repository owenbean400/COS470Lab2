South Korea reported 1,030 new cases of Covid 19 on Saturday , its highest number since the pandemic began , according to the Korea Disease Control and Prevention Agency ( KDCA ) . 
Over 1,000 of the cases were locally transmitted and 28 were imported . 
More than 780 cases were in the Seoul Metropolitan area . 
South Korea has now reported 42,766 cases in total and 580 deaths , including two from Saturday . 
There are 10,372 people in quarantine in the country , according to KDCA .
