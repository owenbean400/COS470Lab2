An evacuation plane carrying 140 South Koreans and their Chinese family members arrived in Seoul today , according to the South Korean foreign ministry . 
The plane had departed Wuhan , where the outbreak began , yesterday . 
This is the third chartered plane South Korea has sent to retrieve its citizens and their families from the Chinese city . 
These evacuees will now be quarantined at a military school facility located in a rural area . 
According to the ministry , there were approximately 2,000 South Korean citizens residing in Wuhan . 
A total of 841 citizens had been repatriated so far through the three chartered flights .
