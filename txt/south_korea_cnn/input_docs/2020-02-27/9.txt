South Korea has reported another death from coronavirus , bringing the national death toll to 13 . 
The death is a 74 year old man who was a member of the Shincheonji religious group . 
He died on Thursday morning , said the mayor of Daegu city , located in the country 's south . 
Many of the cases nationwide have been linked to the Shincheonji branch in Daegu . 
The group says it has handed the list of all its participants to South Korean health authorities .
