Two more South Koreans have tested positive for the novel coronavirus , bringing the national total to 18 , according to the South Korean Centers for Disease Control and Prevention ( KCDC ) . 
The 17th patient is a 38 year old man who visited Singapore between January 18-24 for a conference . 
One of the attendees at the conference was an infected Malaysian citizen . 
The South Korean tested positive this morning , according to the KCDC . 
The 18th patient is a 21 year old woman , the daughter of the 16th confirmed patient who had been in quarantine . 
She tested positive today as well .
