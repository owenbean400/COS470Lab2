The Southern District of New York is banning visitors who have recently traveled to specific countries from the courthouse . 
The ban applies to ... . 
People who have visited to China , South Korea , Japan , Italy and Iran in the last 14 days Others who have had contact in the past two weeks with those who have traveled to those areas Anyone who has been asked to self quarantine Anyone diagnosed with COVID 19 There is no expiration for the order . 
Anyone attempting to enter in violation of the order will be denied entry by a court security officer , according to the order .
