South Korea reported a spike in coronavirus cases Saturday , with 146 new infections confirmed , according to the country ’s Centers for Disease Control and Prevention . 
Previously this week , between 64 and 104 cases have been reported each day . 
The national total , which includes patients who have been treated and discharged , stands at 9,478 . 
Of the 146 new cases , 24 are from airport screenings , while 92 were from areas with previous high concentrations of the virus – the city of Daegu and Gyeonggi Province . 
The remaining 30 were from Seoul and other parts of the country .
