All schools in South Korea will be suspended for another two weeks , the country 's education minister said today . 
The semester had already been postponed by a week -- it now is not scheduled to start until March 23 . 
Yesterday , the Office of Education in Daegu -- the heart of the national outbreak -- delayed the start of school across the city for two more weeks , until March 23 . 
There are now 4,212 cases of the virus and 26 deaths in South Korea , with the majority in Daegu and the surrounding province of North Gyeongsang . 
More than half of the cases are linked to the Shincheonji religious group .
