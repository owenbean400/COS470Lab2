Australia will partially reopen Bondi Beach for swimming and surfing on April 28 , authorities announced Wednesday . 
Paula Masselos , the mayor of Waverley , said access will be provided for surfers and ocean swimmers between 7 a.m . and 5 p.m . on weekdays . 
All land based activities on the beach like jogging , sunbathing and gathering socially is still suspended . 
The new measures will also be applied to Bronte Beach in eastern Sydney .
