Push by reporters on why he has not been tested for coronavirus after coming in contact with someone who tested positive for coronavirus , President Trump said , " I did not say I was not going to be tested . " 
He followed up that " most likely " he 'll get tested but did not say when .

Trump was in close physical proximity with the Wajngarten Saturday night in Florida , two people familiar tell CNN . 
The Brazilian press secretary attended the dinner Trump hosted at his Mar a Lago resort in Florida , took a photo with the President and later stood feet away from Trump as he spoke during Kimberly Guilfoyle 's birthday , the sources said .
