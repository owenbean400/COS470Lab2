Apple plans to close seven stores in Texas again due to an uptick in coronavirus cases in the state . 
The following stores in the Houston area will be closing again : Highland Village , First Colony Mall , Houston Galleria , Memorial City , Willowbrook Mall , Baybrook , and The Woodlands . 
Some context : Apple reopened dozens of its 271 United States stores last month after shutting them because of the coronavirus . 
But last week , Apple decided to once again shutter 11 locations in Florida , North Carolina , South Carolina and Arizona due to a surge in cases in those states .
