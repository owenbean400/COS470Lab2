The Florida county where Disney World is located says it does not plan to revisit the park 's reopening plan , despite pushback from some Disney employees . 
More than 7,000 people are petitioning both Disney and government officials to delay reopening Disney World next month as coronavirus cases surge in Florida . 
The Disney theme park plans to begin a phased reopening on July 11 for its Magic Kingdom and Animal Kingdom parks and July 15 for EPCOT and Hollywood Studios . 
Florida reported an additional 3,286 cases of coronavirus on Tuesday , bringing the state total to 103,503 .
