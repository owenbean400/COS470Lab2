Broward County in Florida announced that two of the three presumptive positive cases of coronavirus in the county are related to people coming through Port Everglades . 
Port Everglades is among the most active ports in the US for cruise ships , cargo and petroleum products . 
The county government is not providing additional information about what exactly connects the cases to Port Everglades . 
The Florida Department of Health has reported 13 cases of coronavirus in the state , including two deaths .
