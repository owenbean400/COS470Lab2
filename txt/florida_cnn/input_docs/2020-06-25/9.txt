At least 29 states are seeing new coronavirus cases increase compared with the prior week . 
That includes the nation 's three most populous states : California , Texas and Florida . 
Among those states , at least 13 are reporting a 50 % increase or higher . 
Another 16 are reporting an increase of between 10 % and 50 % compared to the previous week . 
There is only one state in the US right now that is reporting at least 50 % decrease in new coronavirus cases : Connecticut .
