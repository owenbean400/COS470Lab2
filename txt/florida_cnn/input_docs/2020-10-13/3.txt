The University of Florida has released an updated number of Covid 19 positive tests among its football student athletes , which indicate that the football team has had five new positive tests over the past week . 
The total number of positive tests the team has seen in the month of October now stands at six The Florida Gators , who are ranked number 10 in the nation , are scheduled to play the defending national champion Louisiana State University Tigers on Saturday at home in Gainesville , Florida .
