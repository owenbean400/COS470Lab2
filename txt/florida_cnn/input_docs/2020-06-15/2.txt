The Women 's National Basketball Association has announced plans to begin a shortened 2020 season in late July . 
The season will be played entirely at the IMG Academy in Bradenton , Florida . 
Instead of the previously scheduled 36 games , each of the league 's 12 teams will play 22 regular season games , followed by the traditional postseason format . 
The WNBA announced that players will still receive full salaries and benefits , despite the abbreviated schedule and the fact that the season will be played without fans in attendance . 
The WNBA also committed to making social justice initiatives a focus during the season .
