Jackson Health System in Miami , Florida , has seen a 46 % increase in Covid 19 patients in the past 10 days , according to a hospital spokesperson . 
On June 8 , Jackson Health reported 104 Covid 19 patients . 
Today , they have 152 . 
Jackson Health has discharged a total of 683 Covid 19 patients since the pandemic began . 
Jackson Health System is a non profit academic medical system .
