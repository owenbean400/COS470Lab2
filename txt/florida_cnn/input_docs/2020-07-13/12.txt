KFC is asking its franchisees in four states with a resurgence of coronavirus cases to stop dine in services . 
The major fast food chain sent the guidance Monday to restaurant operators in Florida , Texas , Oklahoma and California . 
KFC is immediately stopping dine in service in 40 Florida restaurants that are owned by the company . 
" This guidance is part of our continued efforts to prioritize the health of our team members , customers , and the communities where they live and work , " a KFC spokesperson told CNN . 
The company said only 5 % of its restaurants in the US have allowed dine in seating since the pandemic began . 
The new guidance does not affect takeout and delivery options .
