A health official in Orange County , Florida , said thus far , they have not seen any Covid 19 outbreaks associated with the reopening of theme parks in the area , Florida Department of Health public information officer Kent Donahue told CNN . 
Donahue told CNN that the health department ’s epidemiology program is in communication with the theme parks . 
Despina Mc Laughlin , public information officer for Orange County , said the county has received a couple of complaints regarding the larger attractions and the use of face coverings . 
Mc Laughlin said the county will not name the attractions and will work together to resolve the issues . 
Mc Laughlin said Orange County ’s Strike Team will continue to visit local businesses , along with large and small theme parks , as part of their daily duties to monitor health protocols .
