Florida Gov . 
Ron De Santis announced a limited reopening of visitations at nursing homes in his state . 
The announcement was made during a roundtable event in Jacksonville . 
De Santis said all visitations will be by appointment only , with residents designating up to 5 visitors each . 
Only 2 visitors will be allowed at one time . 
No minors will be allowed . 
All visitors will have to wear personal protective equipment and pass a screening test , per De Santis . 
And no facility can allow visitors unless 14 days have passed without the onset of a new positive Covid 19 case , in either a resident or a staff member . 
Visitations at nursing homes and long term care facilities were shut down in March by De Santis .
