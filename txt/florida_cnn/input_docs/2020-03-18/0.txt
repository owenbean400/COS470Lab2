Florida Gov . 
Ron De Santis , speaking at a news conference Tuesday , said that all remaining testing for students K 12 has been canceled for the year . 
Requirements for students who are readying for graduation will still be looked at , without the testing component . 
Parents will have a choice to keep their children in the same grade for the 2020-2021 school year . 
There will be no K 12 grades calculated for the rest of this year , he said .
