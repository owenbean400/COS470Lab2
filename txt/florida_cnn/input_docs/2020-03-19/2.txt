All legislature members in the US state of Georgia , along with their staff , are being asked to self quarantine for 14 days after a senator was confirmed to have the coronavirus . 
Sen . 
Brandon Beach has tested positive , the Georgia House said in a statement . 
Meanwhile , two members of the US Congress have tested positive for the virus -- Rep . 
Ben Mc Adams of Utah and Rep . 
Mario Diaz Balart of Florida . 
Several other Congress members in different states have also announced self quarantines after coming in contact with Mc Adams or Diaz Balart .
