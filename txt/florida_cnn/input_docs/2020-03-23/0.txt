Florida Gov . 
Ron De Santis said today that in the next 48 hours the Department of Emergency Management will send out more than 200,000 personal protection equipment items to facilities across the state in response to the Covid 19 outbreak . 
The items include : Almost 60,000 N95 masks 141,000 procedure masks Nearly 27,000 shields 22,000 gowns 28,000 gloves
