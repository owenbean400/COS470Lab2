Florida Gov . 
Ron De Santis just issued an executive order to reduce crowds in restaurants , bars , nightclubs and beaches in response to the coronavirus outbreak . 
Here 's how it breaks down , according to the governor 's press release : Bars and nightclubs : They are closed for the next 30 days . 
Beaches : All parties accessing public beaches in the state of Florida must follow the CDC guidance by limiting their gatherings to no more than 10 people . 
Restaurants : They will now be required to limit customer entry to 50 % capacity . 
Seating must be staggered and limited to ensure guests are separated by a distance of at least six feet , in accordance with CDC guidelines . 
Restaurants are encouraged to remain open and expand take out and delivery services .
