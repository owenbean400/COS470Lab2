Florida Surgeon General Scott Rivkees said social distancing would be the new normal until a vaccine is developed . 
Despite both Rivkees and De Santis saying at the governor ’s briefing that the state is experiencing a plateau , Jared Moskowitz , the state ’s director of emergency management , noted that the current models forecast the peak will occur on April 26 . 
" The model has changed on Florida three times , " Moskowitz said . 
" It was May 4 , then the 21st of April and now it ’s the 26th of April .
