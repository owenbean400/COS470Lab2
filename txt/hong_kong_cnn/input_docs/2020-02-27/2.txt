Health officials in Hong Kong have confirmed an additional case of the novel coronavirus bringing the city wide total to 92 . 
In a press conference on Thursday , Dr . Chuang Shuk Kwan said the latest case is a 70 year old female , who developed a fever earlier this month . 
Dr . Chuang added that the newly confirmed patient had previously visited the Fook Wai Ching Buddhist temple , which has now been connected to several other confirmed cases in the city . 
Of the 63 patients still receiving treatment in hospital , four patients are in serious condition and one patient is in critical condition receiving treatment in an intensive care unit .
