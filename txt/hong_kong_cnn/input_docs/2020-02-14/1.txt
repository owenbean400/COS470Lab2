Hong Kong confirmed three additional coronavirus cases on Thursday , bringing the total to up to 53 , according to a government statement . 
A 43 year old male patient is the relative of a previous case . 
He developed a cough on Jan . 29 , and has had diarrhea and vomiting since Jan . 31 . 
The other two new cases are a 67 year old woman and her 37 year old son , who had dinner with three previous confirmed cases in a seafood restaurant . 
The mother developed a cough on Jan . 31 and a fever on Feb . 1 , while her son developed a fever on Feb . 8 .
