Japan recorded 649 new coronavirus cases on Saturday , bringing its total caseload to 75,980 , the Japanese Health Ministry reported Sunday . 
The country also reported 16 new deaths on Saturday . 
So far , 1,451 people have died from the coronavirus in Japan , and 190 patients remain critically ill with the virus , according to the ministry . 
Japan has been grappling with a second wave of infections since July , with more than 1,000 cases recorded daily during its peak in August . 
Its capital Tokyo is among the worst hit cities . 
On Saturday , Tokyo recorded 226 new cases , bringing the city 's total number of infections to 22,857 .
