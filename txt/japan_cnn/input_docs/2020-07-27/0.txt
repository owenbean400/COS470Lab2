Japan 's health ministry recorded 839 new coronavirus cases on Sunday , raising the total number of cases reported nationwide to 30,701 . 
The death toll stands at 1,009 . 
Of Sunday 's new cases , 239 were from Tokyo . 
It marks the sixth consecutive day that the capital has reported more than 200 new cases . 
Meanwhile , the city of Osaka reported 141 new cases . 
Case numbers have been rising in Japan in recent weeks , with the virus spreading to US military bases in Okinawa prefecture . 
On Saturday , authorities confirmed 64 new cases on the bases .
