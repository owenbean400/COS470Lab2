Japan recorded 746 new Covid 19 cases on Sunday , the country 's Ministry of Health , Labor and Welfare said today . 
The national total for coronavirus infections is now 63,219 , according to the government . 
Five new deaths were reported on Sunday , bringing the total death toll to 1,194 . 
Among the new cases , 212 were reported in Tokyo . 
According to the city government , 51 % of the new cases on Sunday were identified in patients aged in their 20s and 30s .
