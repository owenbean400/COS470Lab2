Another 218 cases of the coronavirus were confirmed in Japan on Sunday , as the total number of infections recorded in the country reached 15,769 . 
According to Japan 's health ministry , 18 people died on Sunday from the virus , bringing the country 's death toll to 523 . 
The totals include 712 cases and 13 deaths linked to the Diamond Princess cruise ship . 
Tokyo reported 91 new cases and four deaths . 
Japan 's capital has now recorded a total of 4,568 cases and 145 fatalities . 
Abe expected to extend state of emergency : Japan 's Prime Minister will make an announcement today on the lockdown measures designed to slow the spread of the virus . 
According to Japan 's public broadcaster NHK , Abe is likely to extend the state of emergency until May 31 .
