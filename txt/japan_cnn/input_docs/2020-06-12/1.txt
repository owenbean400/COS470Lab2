Formula One Grand Prix Races in Japan and Singapore have been canceled because of the coronavirus pandemic , according to a statement on Formula One 's website . 
It was confirmed today that " Azerbaijan , Singapore , and Japanese races will not be part of the calendar for 2020 , " the statement says . 
The 2020 season will start with two races at the Austrian Grand Prix , the first being on July 5 , followed a week later by a second race on the same track . 
All racing was put on hold because of the spread of novel coronavirus across the world . 
This post has been updated to clarify the dates of races in Austria .
