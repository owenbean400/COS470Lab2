Japan had 261 new coronavirus cases and 26 deaths yesterday , according to the country 's health ministry . 
The nationwide state of emergency was set to lift on May 6 , but Prime Minster Shinzo Abe said on Friday that the government plans to extend it for about a month . 
The final decision will be made Monday , he said . 
Japan has had a total of 15,257 coronavirus cases and 471 deaths .
