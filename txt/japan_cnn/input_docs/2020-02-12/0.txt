The virus : The novel coronavirus has killed more than 1,100 people and infected over 45,000 people worldwide . 
The vast majority of cases are in mainland China . 
Cruise ship crisis : A total of 175 cases have been confirmed on a cruise ship quarantined in Japan . 
More than 2,000 people are stranded on another cruise that was turned away from four ports , despite having no suspected cases . 
Life in China : People are returning to work after entire cities and business sectors were shut down for more than two weeks in efforts to contain the outbreak . 
In Hubei province — the outbreak 's epicenter — millions remain under lockdown .
