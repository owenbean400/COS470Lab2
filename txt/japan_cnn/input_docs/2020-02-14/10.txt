Two more Japanese people have tested positive for the novel coronavirus , according to an announcement from the country 's health ministry . 
One is a man in his 60s from Aicha Prefecture , the other a man in his 50s from Hokkaido . 
Neither has a history of travel to China , the ministry said . 
That makes seven new Japanese cases today not from the Diamond Princess cruise ship , which is quarantined in Yokohama . 
It brings the total in Japan to 257 , of which 219 are from the vessel .
