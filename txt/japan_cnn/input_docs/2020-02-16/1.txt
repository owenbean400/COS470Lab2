Another 142 people died from coronavirus in mainland China on Saturday , the country 's National Health Commission said today . 
That brings the worldwide total of deaths to 1,669 , all but four of which occurred in mainland China . 
By the end of Saturday , 68,500 cases of the virus had been recorded in mainland China , an increase of 2,009 from the previous day . 
Globally , there have been 69,000 confirmed cases , with the largest outbreaks outside of mainland China being reported in Japan , Singapore and Hong Kong . 
So far , almost 9,500 patients with the coronavirus have been treated and discharged from hospitals across China , the commission said .
