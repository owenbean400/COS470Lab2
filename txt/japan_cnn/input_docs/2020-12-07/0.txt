Japan 's government is set to dispatch nurses from the country 's Self Defense Forces to respond to a request for medical staff to deal with a rise in critically ill Covid 19 patients , according to Chief Cabinet Secretary Katsunobu Kato . 
The additional medical staff are needed in Osaka and Hokkaido prefectures . 
Osaka reported 310 positive cases on Sunday , its sixth straight day of more than 300 new daily cases . 
Critical cases in the city now occupy almost 70 % of available beds for treating severe Covid 19 symptoms . 
Japan 's Health Ministry announced 2,016 new coronavirus infections and 20 deaths on Sunday . 
In total , Japan has reported more than 162,000 cases , including more than 2,200 deaths .
