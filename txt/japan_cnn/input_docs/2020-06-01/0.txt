Japan recorded 33 new coronavirus infections and one virus related death on Sunday , according to the country 's health ministry . 
The new cases bring Japan 's nationwide total to 17,597 confirmed cases and 905 fatalities . 
Of those , 712 cases and 13 deaths were tied to the Diamond Princess cruise ship . 
Tokyo recorded five new patients on Sunday -- the first time in six days that the number of new cases there was below double digits . 
The Japanese capital is currently in step two of its coronavirus recovery plan , which means that schools and some commercial facilities are allowed to reopen .
