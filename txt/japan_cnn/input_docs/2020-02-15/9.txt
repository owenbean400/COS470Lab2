There are nine more people in Japan , not on the Diamond Princess ship , who have tested positive for the novel coronavirus , the Japanese Mistry of Health , Labour , and Welfare said Saturday . 
One person is a surgeon in his 50s who works at same hospital where another doctor tested positive earlier , in Wakayama Prefecture . 
Eight others are in Tokyo . 
Seven of them had close contact with a taxi driver who was found to be infected , the ministry said .
