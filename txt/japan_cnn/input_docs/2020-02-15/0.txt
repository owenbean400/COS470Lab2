Japan announced a total of eight new coronavirus cases on Friday , that are not from the Diamond Princess ship docked under quarantine in Yokohama Bay . 
The eighth case is a male Yokohama city officer in his 30s , who helped an infected patient from the Diamond Princess on February 10 . 
He was wearing protective gear and goggles . 
The health ministry is looking into how he contract the virus . 
It brings the total number of cases in Japan to 258 , of which 219 are from the Diamond Princess . 
One patient has died in Japan so far .
