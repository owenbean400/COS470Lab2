Japan recorded 270 new Covid 19 cases on Monday , the lowest daily spike since July 13 before the country was hit by a second wave of infections , according to the Health Ministry . 
Nine new deaths were also recorded Monday , bringing the total death toll to 1,464 , with the total caseload at 76,670 , the Health Ministry said . 
Eighty new cases were recorded in Tokyo on Monday , marking the first time the capital 's daily new infections fell below 100 in eight days , according to the Tokyo metropolitan government . 
The city has reported a total of 23,083 cases . 
As daily infections drop , authorities are planning to ease restrictions . 
In central Tokyo , the 10 p.m . closure request for bars and restaurants that serve alcohol will end today .
