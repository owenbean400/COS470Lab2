Diamond Princess cruise ship captain Stefano Ravera has just made an announcement onboard the cruise liner , reporting that two passengers have died of the novel coronavirus , as heard on a video recording supplied to CNN by a passenger . 
Ravera read out a Princess Cruises statement : The two passengers who died were in their 80s and had been in hospital in Japan , but no further information is known about them at this stage . 
There are 624 confirmed cases of the virus linked to the stricken ship , docked in Yokohama , Japan . 
Passengers who tested negative are still disembarking from the ship today .
