Japan reported 1,579 new Covid 19 cases and five deaths on Friday , according to the health ministry . 
It 's the third day in a row that the country has reported its highest daily infections . 
The total number of confirmed cases in Japan stands at 36,548 , with 712 from the Diamond Princess cruise ship . 
There have been 1,024 deaths , with 13 from the cruise ship . 
The capital Tokyo also reported its highest daily increase on Friday , with 463 cases . 
That brings the total number of infections in the city to 12,691 , with more than half of those from July . 
Koike urged Tokyo residents to avoid unnecessary trips in summer holiday in August .
