Japan on Thursday lifted the state of emergency in three prefectures : Osaka , Kyoto and Hyogo . 
The state of emergency -- enacted to curb the spread of coronavirus -- remains in place for five hardest hit regions , including Tokyo and Hokkaido . 
The government will convene on Monday to decide whether to lift the order in those places The government had imposed a nationwide state of emergency on April 16 , and lifted it for most of the country last Thursday . 
New cases : Japan recorded 43 new coronavirus cases and 19 deaths on Thursday , according to the health ministry . 
That raises the national total to 17,051 cases and 809 deaths . 
Of those , more than 700 cases and at least 13 deaths are linked to the Diamond Princess cruise ship , which was docked in quarantine at Yokohama Bay for two weeks in February .
