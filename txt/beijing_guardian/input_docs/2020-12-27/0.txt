The first delivery of Sinovac ’s Covid 19 vaccine to Turkey has been delayed because of a coronavirus outbreak in Beijing customs , the country ’s health minister has said . 
Fahrettin Koca said the shipment would be a day or two late , after a member of staff was confirmed as having the virus . 
Turkey has agreed to buy 50 million doses of the firm ’s vaccine , and 3 million were due to arrive on Monday .
