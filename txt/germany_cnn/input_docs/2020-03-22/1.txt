The number of coronavirus cases in Germany has increased by 1,948 in the past 24 hours to 18,610 , according to the country ’s agency for disease control , the Robert Koch Institute . 
That jump represents a 12 % increase on Saturday ’s figures . 
Meanwhile , the number of deaths has increased by eight to 55 . 
The German government is considering a nationwide lockdown to combat coronavirus . 
Last week , two German states -- Bavaria and Saarland -- imposed state wide restrictions to try to contain its spread . 
German Chancellor Angela Merkel is set to meet state premiers later on Sunday to discuss further measures .
