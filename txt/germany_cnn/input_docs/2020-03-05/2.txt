Poland has confirmed its first case of the coronavirus , the Polish Minister of Health announced on Wednesday . 
According to Minister Lukasz Szumowski , the patient came to Poland from Germany . 
He is in hospital in a stable condition , and all those who came in contact with him have been quarantined . 
A total of 584 coronavirus tests have been carried out in Poland , and 200 people who have been in contact with suspected patients remain under quarantine , Szumowski said .
