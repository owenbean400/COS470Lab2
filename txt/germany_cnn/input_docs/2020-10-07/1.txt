The city of Frankfurt , Germany has introduced a slew of new social distancing measures and public health restrictions , including an alcohol ban and a curfew , to contain a surge in new coronavirus cases . 
Alcohol will no longer be consumed in public spaces , and a curfew will be enforced from 10 p.m . until 6 a.m . Gatherings in public and rented spaces will be limited to 25 people , and only 10 will be allowed to meet in private . 
Masks will be mandatory in malls , major shopping streets and at religious ceremonies . 
Authorities are also considering making them mandatory in upper schools after the autumn holiday .
