The German government has asked the public to refrain from joking about the coronavirus on April Fool 's Day today , declaring that " Corona is no joke . " 
It is usually a tradition in Western countries such as Germany for people to make up stories and pull pranks on April 1 . 
The ministry added it was important to avoid making jokes about the virus to " minimize the danger that the fight against the virus will be made more difficult by false information on the subject . ' ' Germany has more than 71,800 cases of the virus , including 775 deaths , according to a tally from Johns Hopkins University .
