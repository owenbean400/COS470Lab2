France ’s Palace of Versailles , one of the country ’s biggest tourist attractions , reopened today , with face masks mandatory , and a one way route imposed . 
80 % of the palace ’s visitors generally come from abroad . 
" This financial model has been devastated . 
We have to start again , " Catherine Pegard , who runs the palace , told Reuters . 
" We ’re not the only ones . " 
The Louvre has also reopened .
