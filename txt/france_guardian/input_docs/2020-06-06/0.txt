The death toll in France has risen to 29,142 from 29,111 yesterday . 
The number in people in hospital with Covid 19 has decreased from 12,696 on Friday to 12,479 today . 
The number of people in intensive care units has also decreased to 1,059 , from 1,094 on Friday . 
The overall number of confirmed coronavirus cases in the country has risen to 153,634 from 153,055 on Friday .
