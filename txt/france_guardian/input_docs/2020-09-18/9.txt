The Victorian government has released its daily update . 
The number of new cases in the past 24 hours was 21 . 
That ’s a drop of more than half on yesterday ’s figure , which was 45 new cases . 
There were seven deaths in the past 24 hours . 
The rolling 14 day average is 39.3 new cases for Melbourne and 1.9 cases for regional Victoria .
