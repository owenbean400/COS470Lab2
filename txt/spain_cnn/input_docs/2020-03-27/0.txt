Spain reached a grim milestone this week with more than 4,000 coronavirus related deaths reported . 
This spike has resulted in the country transforming an ice skating rink into a makeshift morgue to help relieve the pressure on hospitals and medical facilities , according to CNN 's Scott Mc Lean . 
Mc Lean shared this insight during CNN 's coronavirus town hall Thursday evening . 
Over the past 24 hours , Mc Lean said Spain has reported 650 deaths . 
That brings the total to more than 4,300 , according to the Johns Hopkins tally .
