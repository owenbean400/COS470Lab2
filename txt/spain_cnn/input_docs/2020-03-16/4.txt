Spain will close its land borders because of the coronavirus pandemic , the country ’s Interior Minister Fernando Grande Marlaska announced Monday . 
Marlaska said the decision was made in coordination with Spain ’s neighbors — Portugal and France — and its objective is to " slow down the curve of infected people . " 
The measures will come into effect at 7 p.m . ET ( which is midnight local time ) on Monday , and will last until the end of the 15 day state of emergency . 
It does not include nationals or residents returning home , cross border workers , diplomats doing their work and other exceptional situations . 
The restrictions wo not affect transporting goods to keep the economy activity , the Interior Minister added .
