Newly reported confirmed cases of the coronavirus in Spain brought the nationwide total to 39 on Friday . 
Of those 39 : 37 are in quarantine ( eight in Andalusia , one in Aragon , two in Castilla Leon , 10 in Valencia , five in the Canary Islands , seven in Madrid and four in Catalonia ) Two have recovered from the illness ( one in Canary Island and one in the Balearic Islands ) .
