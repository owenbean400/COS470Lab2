The Chinese president , Xi Jinping , has held a special government meeting to address the spread of the virus . 
The president warned of a " grave situation " at a meeting with the politburo on the lunar new year public holiday , state television reported . 
He also said the virus was " accelerating its speed " . 
According to the TV report , resources and experts will be channelled to designated hospitals to tackle the virus . 
Supplies to Hubei province will be guaranteed and cost wo not be a hindrance .
