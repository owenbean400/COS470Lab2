In China , 30 provinces , municipalities and autonomous regions have now raised their public health alert to level 1 , the highest level , according to Global Times . 
This enables measures such as quarantining . 
Sichuan province has announced a ban on group gatherings . 
Individuals are not allowed to organise gatherings and restaurants are being prevented from hosting them . 
Anyone who had prior reservations has been told to cancel .
