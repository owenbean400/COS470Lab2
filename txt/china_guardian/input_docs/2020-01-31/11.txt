On Thursday , China ’s Football Association cancelled all domestic games at all levels , in a response to the coronavirus . 
China ’s national women ’s team are in Australia to play Olympic qualifiers , but have been in quarantine in Brisbane as a result of the virus . 
Football Federation Australia on Friday confirmed the team will begin their qualifying tournament against Taiwan in Sydney on 6 February and will play the Australian team on 12 February .
