US authorities are accusing Chinese hackers of trying to steal Covid 19 vaccine research , Agence France Presse ( AFP ) reports . 
The FBI and the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency ( CISA ) warned : China ’s efforts to target these sectors pose a significant threat to our nation ’s response to Covid 19 . 
Neither offered evidence or examples to support the allegation . 
Washington has increasingly blamed Beijing for the outbreak caused by the virus that first emerged in China late last year . 
Asked on Monday about earlier reports that the US believed Chinese hackers were targeting US vaccine research , the president Donald Trump replied : What else is new with China ?
