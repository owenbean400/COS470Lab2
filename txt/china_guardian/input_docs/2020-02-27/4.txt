Fiji has extended its travel ban due to coronavirus fears . 
Travellers who have been in Italy , Iran and the South Korean cities of Daegu and Cheongdo will not be permitted to enter Fiji . 
Visitors who had been in mainland China in the last 14 days have also been forbidden entry into the Pacific nation . 
There are no suspected or confirmed cases of coronavirus in Fiji , but Pacific nations are fearful of how their health systems will cope were the virus to reach their shores .
