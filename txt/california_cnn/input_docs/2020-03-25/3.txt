The number of cases in California is climbing steadily and now stands at 2,535 , with 53 deaths , according to Gov . 
Gavin Newsom . 
Of these positive cases , over half those infected are between the ages of 18 and 49 and 37 are under the age of 18 . 
The governor is urging people to abide by stay at home orders , imploring the public that " half way is no way . " 
" We can defeat this virus , but we ca not defeat it unless we commit to fulfilling our individual obligations and our collective responsibilities to meet this moment . 
The stay home order is real , " Newsom said . 
" Do not think for a second that we ’re a day or two away from lifting that order , we ’re not even a week or two away . "
