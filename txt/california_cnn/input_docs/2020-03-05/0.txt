Santa Clara County in Northern California has three new cases of the novel coronavirus , the county ’s public health department confirmed on its website today . 
One case is from an adult male who is currently hospitalized , exposure details are under investigation . 
The other two cases , both adult males , are close contacts of an existing case . 
Both are in isolation at home , according to Santa Clara County ’s Public Health Department . 
By the numbers : There are now 14 cases in Santa Clara County . 
California has 36 cases , including one death .
