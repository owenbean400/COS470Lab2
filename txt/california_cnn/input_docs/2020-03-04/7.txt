The health department in King County , Washington , confirms that one of the nine novel coronavirus deaths reported in its county was a resident of Life Care Center in Kirkland . 
This is the sixth resident of that nursing home to die in the coronavirus outbreak . 
A health department statement describes the latest victim as a woman in her 90s who died on Tuesday at Evergreen Health in Kirkland . 
By the numbers : As of Wednesday afternoon , there are 11 total US deaths attributed to the novel coronavirus ; 
10 in Washington state and one in California .
