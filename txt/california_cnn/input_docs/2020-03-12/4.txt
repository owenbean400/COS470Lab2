American passengers from the Grand Princess cruise ship , which was docked in California after infections were found onboard , landed early Thursday local time at Dobbins Air Reserve Base in Marietta , Georgia . 
The passengers had been screened by the Department of Health and Human Services and US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention ( CDC ) before arriving . 
They will now begin a 14 day quarantine as a precaution , in case they were exposed to the coronavirus aboard the ship . 
The CDC is responsible for all aspects of the quarantine operation , and Dobbins personnel will have no contact with these passengers The passengers who landed today are part of the second wave -- some passengers had been sent to Dobbins earlier this week , while others were sent to the Travis Air Force Base in California for their quarantine .
