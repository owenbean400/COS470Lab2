Naval Base Ventura County in Oxnard , California might be tasked with receiving coronavirus evacuees , according to a statement from NBVC Point Mugu , Port Hueneme and San Nicolas Island . 
" Secretary of Defense Mark T . Esper has tasked Naval Base Ventura County with supporting the potential repatriation of American citizens subject to federal quarantine due to the coronavirus . 
A Community Info Session is scheduled Monday , Feb . 24 , 6:30 p.m . at the Pt . 
Mugu Theater , " read the post on the NBVC Facebook page .
