Los Angeles International Airport ( LAX ) has received its first shipment of the Pfizer Covid 19 vaccine , the airport tweeted on Sunday night . 
" This is a major milestone for science , our country and our community . 
Thank you to all those who made this delivery possible , and are part of the incredible effort to distribute vaccines around the world , " LAX tweeted shortly before 10 p.m . PT . 
Running out of hospital beds : California reported 30,334 new Covid 19 cases on Sunday and 122 virus related deaths , according to the state 's Department of Public Health ( CDPH ) . 
Hospitals are filling up across the state ; 
ICU capacity is down to 4.2 % in Southern California , and even lower at 1.5 % in the San Joaquin Valley .
