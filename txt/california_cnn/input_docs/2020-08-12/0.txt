As Texas soars past 500,000 Covid 19 cases , state officials are redoubling their efforts to get residents to wear masks and practice social distancing , after suggesting that the surge has been driven by people letting their guard down . 
Texas has been at the center of the resurgence of coronavirus cases along with California and Florida , which are both reporting thousands of new daily cases . 
Though Texas officials initially came under scrutiny for downplaying the severity of the virus ' spread and acting slowly to pull back plans for reopening , Abbott said during a news conference Tuesday that the state is ramping up distribution of personal protective equipment and encouraging Texans to wear masks and socially distance .
