Millions of N95 respirator masks have been certified and will soon be shipped to California , according to a statement from Gov . 
Gavin Newsom . 
The masks were part of a somewhat secretive deal made between the state and BYD during the early days of the coronavirus pandemic . 
BYD , an electric car maker based in China , stands for Build Your Dreams . 
The contract calls for 150 million N95 masks . 
The masks were held up pending certification from the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health .
