California will be offering two weeks of supplemental paid sick leave for people in the food industry who have contracted Covid 19 or have been exposed to it , Gov . 
Gavin Newsom announced today . 
" We do not want you going to work if you ’re sick , " Newsom said . 
" We want to make sure that you know that if you ’re sick , it ’s okay to acknowledge it and it ’s okay to let your employer know and still know that you ’re going to get a supplemental paycheck for a minimum of two weeks , " he added .
