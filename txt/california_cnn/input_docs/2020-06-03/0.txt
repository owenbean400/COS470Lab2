California Gov . 
Gavin Newsom signed an executive order today requiring mail in ballots be sent to all registered voters in the state for the upcoming general election in November . 
The executive order cites the health risks posed by the Covid 19 pandemic and the potential of spreading the virus through in person voting . 
In addition to mail in ballots , county officials are directed to provide voting locations remain available for those wishing to cast their ballots in person . 
At least one voting location per 10,000 registered voters is required .
