A third case of the Wuhan coronavirus has been confirmed in the United States , authorities said . 
The individual traveled from Wuhan to Orange County , California . 
He is now being held in isolation in a local hospital and is in good condition , according to the Orange County Health Care Agency 's ( OCHCA ) Communicable Disease Control Division . 
The other two confirmed cases in the US that have been identified are in Washington state and Illinois .
