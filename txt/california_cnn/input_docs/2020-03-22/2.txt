California Governor Gavin Newsom is telling young people at beaches that the outbreak is not the time to party -- it 's time to stay home . 
" Do not be selfish , " Newsom said , adding that for young people " thinking this is a party , ( it 's ) time to grow up , " He encouraged everyone to stay at home to combat the coronavirus outbreak , saying that it was best to assume you are contagious and distance yourself from others .
