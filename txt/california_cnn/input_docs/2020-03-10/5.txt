A commercial lab in San Juan Capistrano is starting to process coronavirus tests , with a capacity of more than 1,200 tests per day , Gov . 
Gavin Newsom announced Tuesday . 
This commercial testing will supplement the 18 state labs that are currently operational . 
More than 1,000 people in California have already been tested and there are 7,675 tests available . 
Two more commercial labs are being brought online and will have the capacity to process in excess of 5,000 tests per day . 
The labs , located in Sacramento and West Hills , will be automated and still need support and approval , but are expected to be online by March 24 , Newsom said .
