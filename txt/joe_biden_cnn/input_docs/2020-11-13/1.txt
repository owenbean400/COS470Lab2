US Senate Majority Leader Mitch Mc Connell on Thursday continued to reject the call from Democrats for a big coronavirus stimulus package , saying , " that 's not a place I think we 're willing to go " and reiterated his push for a bill that 's " highly targeted at what the residual problems are . " 
Mc Connell said of House Speaker Nancy Pelosi and Sen . 
Chuck Schumer , " I gather she and the Democratic leader in the Senate still are looking at something dramatically larger . 
That 's not a place I think we 're willing to go . " 
Pelosi and Schumer signaled during a news conference earlier on Thursday they 're not willing to change their call for a large coronavirus package even as efforts to negotiate remain stalled . 
The Democratic leaders instead argued that the result of the presidential election was a mandate for Joe Biden , which in turn strengthens their position in an effort to get a Covid package in the lame duck session .
