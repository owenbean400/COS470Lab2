Britain is at a critical point in the Covid 19 pandemic and faces a very challenging winter , one of the government ’s top medical advisers will warn at a public briefing on Monday , according to Reuters . 
Cases in Britain are on the increase in what Boris Johnson has labelled a second wave of the virus , with large areas of the country subject to restrictions on social freedom and London expected to be next in line."The trend in the UK is heading in the wrong direction and we are at a critical point in the pandemic , " England ’s Chief Medical Officer Chris Whitty is expected to say in an address set for 10am on Monday."We are looking at the data to see how to manage the spread of the virus ahead of a very challenging winter period."More than 40,000 people in Britain have died within 28 days of testing positive for Covid 19 since the start of the pandemic in March , the highest number in Europe .
