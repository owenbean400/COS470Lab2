An unidentified player at the Serie A club Torino has tested positive in the first round of testing before the side ’s planned return to training on Friday . 
Serie A teams have begun returning to training this week after Italy ’s eight week lockdown , the longest in Europe , although there has still been no decision on when , or if , the season can start again . 
The club said : During the first medical examinations carried out on the players of Torino , a positive result emerged for Covid 19 . 
The player , currently asymptomatic , was immediately placed in quarantine and will be constantly monitored .
