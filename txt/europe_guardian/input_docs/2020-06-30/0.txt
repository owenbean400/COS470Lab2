Sweden is to set up a commission to evaluate the its response to the pandemic , according to Agence France Presse ( AFP ) . 
The country has taken a softer approach than the rest of Europe . 
Its prime minister Stefan Lofven said : The ability of the entire community to come together is one of Sweden ’s great strengths . 
But , at the same time , this crisis has put a spotlight on shortfalls in our society . 
The committee would be " tasked with evaluating the government ’s , the authorities ’ , the health regions ’ and municipalities ’ measures to curb the spread of the coronavirus and the effects of the spread , " Lofven added .
