Brazil ’s health ministry reported 46,860 new cases of novel coronavirus on Friday -- bringing the country 's total to at least 1,274,974 . 
The ministry also reported 990 additional related deaths , taking Brazil 's toll to 55,961 . 
Sao Paulo , Brazil ’s most populous state and the epicenter of its Covid 19 outbreak , has at least 258,508 confirmed cases . 
At least 13,966 people have died from the virus there , according to the state health secretary . 
Rio de Janeiro state has at least 108,497 cases and at least 9,587 deaths .
