Mexico has recorded 4,448 new cases of the novel coronavirus on Sunday , taking the total number of cases in the country to 522,162 . 
The Health Ministry also added 214 new deaths for a total count of 56,757 . 
Mexico continues to hold the third highest number of deaths in the world from coronavirus following only the US and Brazil , according to Johns Hopkins University ( JHU ) . 
Mexico is now ranked third in Latin America by JHU in terms of its number of total coronavirus cases . 
As of Sunday , Mexico trails Peru - which has 525,803 total cases - by nearly 4,000 cases and Brazil , which has recorded more than 3.3 million infections .
