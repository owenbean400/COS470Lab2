Coronavirus infections in Latin America and the Caribbean surpassed 3 million this week , according to Johns Hopkins University data , with Brazil , Peru and Chile reporting the highest number of infections . 
Dr . Carissa Etienne , director of the Pan American Health Organization ( PAHO ) , expressed concern about unsafe working conditions and the lack of protective equipment for frontline health workers in much of the region . 
She reiterated the need for better tracing of the virus and said its impact had been worsened by inequality , political division and under investment in health care .
