Russia reported 8,764 new cases of coronavirus on Wednesday , bringing the country 's total to 308,705 , according to the Russian coronavirus response headquarters . 
It also recorded 135 deaths in the past day -- the highest daily increase so far . 
The national death toll stands at 2,972 . 
Russia is the second worst hit country in the world , following the United States . 
Its numbers have surged in recent weeks , going from a few thousand cases per day in April to 10,000 cases per day in May . 
The US is sending ventilators and other aid to Russia , which are expected to ship today , according to an administration spokesperson .
