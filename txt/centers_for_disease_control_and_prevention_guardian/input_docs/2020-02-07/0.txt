Passengers on a cruise ship that docked in New York City on Friday morning are being tested for the coronavirus , it has been confirmed . 
An official from the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention ( CDC ) told CNN : There are folks on the ship that have a history of travel to China and so CDC and local health officials are going to board the ship when it docks to do an assessment in port for coronavirus . 
Video footage showed ambulances and stretchers on standby near the port at Bayonne , and personnel boarding the ship .
