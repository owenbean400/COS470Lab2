New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said he is going to ask the President to shift the USNS Comfort " from non Covid to Covid . " 
The Navy ship was initially dispatched to New York to treat non coronavirus patients . 
" We do not need the comfort for non Covid cases , " the governor said . 
The Comfort is outfitted with 1,000 hospital beds and is currently docked in New York City . 
Cuomo said utilizing those beds for coronavirus patients would " make a significant difference " for the state . 
Cuomo said he is going to call Trump today to put in the request .
