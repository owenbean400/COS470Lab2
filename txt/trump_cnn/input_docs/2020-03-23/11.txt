US stocks ended in the red on Monday , after the Senate failed for a second time to vote through the coronavirus economic relief package . 
A slew of new stimulus measures from the Federal Reserve provided a boost to premarket trading , but it did not help stocks end the regular trading day higher . 
The Dow finished down 3 % , or 583 points . 
The S&P 500 closed down 2.9 % . 
The index has now erased all of the gains accumulated under the Trump administration . 
The Nasdaq Composite finished 0.3 % lower .
