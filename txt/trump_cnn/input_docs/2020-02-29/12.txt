Dr . Robert Redfield , director of the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention , tweeted that the agency gave incorrect information about the Washington coronavirus patient who died to President Trump and Vice President Mike Pence on Saturday . 
The patient who died was a man in his 50s in Washington state . 
This was the first death from the novel coronavirus in the United States . 
Trump and other officials , including Redfield , identified the patient as a woman in her 50s during a press briefing on Saturday . 
A Seattle and King County health official later said the patient was a man .
