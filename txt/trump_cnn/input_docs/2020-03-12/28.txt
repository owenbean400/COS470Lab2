President Trump said today that domestic travel restrictions due to the novel coronavirus have not been discussed , but they remain a possibility . 
" Are you considering travel restrictions within the United States , such as to Washington state or to California ? " a reporter asked the President during an Oval Office meeting between Trump and Irish Taoiseach Leo Varadkar . 
" You see what they ’re doing in New Rochelle , which is good , frankly , but it ’s not enforced , " Trump said of the New York area being impacted by the virus . 
" It ’s not very strong , but people know they ’re being watched . "
