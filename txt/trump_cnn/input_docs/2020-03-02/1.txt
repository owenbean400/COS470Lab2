President Trump said he 's asked researchers to accelerate development of a coronavirus vaccine . 
" We asked them to accelerate whatever they 're doing in terms of the vaccine , absolutely , " Trump said moments ago . More than 20 potential vaccines aimed at preventing coronavirus disease are in development around the world , the World Health Organization 's director general said Friday . 
But health officials have consistently said it will take at least a year before any vaccine is proven effective and gets necessary approvals for wide distribution .
