Vice President Mike Pence said Friday the Constitutional rights to speech and free assembly explained the push to hold campaign rallies even as coronavirus cases surge . 
" We have an election coming up this fall . 
And President Trump and I believe that taking proper steps ... and giving people the very best counsel that we have , we still want to give people the freedom to participate in the political process . 
And we respect that , " he added . 
Trump held a campaign rally last weekend in Tulsa . 
Afterward multiple campaign staffers tested positive for coronavirus . 
He later spoke to a large crowd in Arizona , one of the states where cases are rising .
