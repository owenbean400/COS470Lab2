The United States will send ventilators to Ecuador , El Salvador and Indonesia , President Trump tweeted Friday , announcing he spoke with the countries ' presidents . 
Trump said he had a " great conversation with President Lenin Moreno " of Ecuador . 
He added that the US " will be sending them desperately needed Ventilators , of which we have recently manufactured many , and helping them in other ways . 
They are fighting hard against Corona Virus ! " 
Trump also praised El Salvador for helping the US on immigration . 
" Will be helping them with Ventilators , which are desperately needed , " Trump wrote . 
" They have worked well with us on immigration at the Southern Border ! "
