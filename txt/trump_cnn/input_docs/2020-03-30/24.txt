President Donald Trump described a grim scene of Elmhurst Hospital in Queens , including body bags and refrigerated trucks to take away bodies . 
" I 've been watching them bringing in trailer trucks . 
Freezer trucks . 
Freezer trucks , because they ca not handle the bodies because there are so many of them , " Trump said .

Trump also described body bags in hospital hallways , but CNN has seen no evidence that there are body bags in the hallways of the hospital . 
There are refrigerated trucks where hospital personnel have been putting bodies to alleviate the capacity of the regular morgue .
