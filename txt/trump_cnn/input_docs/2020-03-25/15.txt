Missouri Gov . 
Mike Parson is asking President Trump to issue a major disaster declaration for the entire state due to coronavirus . 
Parson hopes a declaration like this would " provide federal assistance for state and local Covid 19 pandemic preparedness and emergency response efforts , " the governor 's office said in a news release . 
Missouri has been in a state of emergency since March 13 . 
There are eight confirmed deaths in the state due to coronavirus .
