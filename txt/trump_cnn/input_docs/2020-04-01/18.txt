When asked Wednesday evening during a coronavirus task force briefing why he did not issue a universal stay at home order for the entirety of the country , President Trump implied that some states are basically fine when it comes to the disease . 
Facts First : The entire country has been impacted by the coronavirus . 
Americans have tested positive in every US state and all but one territory . 
As of the start of Wednesday 's press briefing , all 50 states had at least 100 cases .
