President Trump says he ’s pushed the Food and Drug Administration to eliminate barriers to getting therapeutics for coronavirus to patients . 
He said a drug currently used as an antimalarial — hydroxychloroquine — would be made available almost immediately to treat coronavirus . 
" It ’s been around for a long time , so we know if things do not go as planned , it ’s not going to kill anybody , " Trump told reporters at the White House today . 
Trump said it was a common drug and would soon be available for " prescribed use " on coronavirus . 
He called it a " game changer . "
