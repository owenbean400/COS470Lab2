Senior Republican Sen . 
Lamar Alexander , who chairs the committee on health , education , labor and pensions , told CNN it would " help " if President Trump wore a mask because it would eliminate political stigma around doing so . 
When asked whether the American people should look to the President for public health advice given his past remarks , including his comment about slowing down testing , the senator said the public should listen to medical experts . 
" My suggestion to the President all along and for the other political leaders is let the experts do the talking about medicine . 
People trust them , " Alexander said .
