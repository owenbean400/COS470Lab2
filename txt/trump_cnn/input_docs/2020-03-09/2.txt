President Trump is continuing to tweet on the coronavirus response . 
He thanked Vice President Pence and the coronavirus task force , and once again praised his decision to shut down travel from parts of the world that had been impacted by the virus . 
" Great job being done by the @ VP and the Corona Virus Task Force . 
Thank you ! " he said in one tweet . 
" The BEST decision made was the toughest of them all - which saved many lives . 
Our VERY early decision to stop travel to and from certain parts of the world ! " he said in another .
