Oklahoma Gov . 
Kevin Stitt said that the state has 352 new positive Covid 19 cases today , just one day ahead of President Trump ’s rally in Tulsa . 
The state has 9,706 total cases , up 3.8 % since Thursday ’s report , Stitt said during a news conference today . 
He also said that 211 people have been hospitalized with symptoms . 
Speaking about Saturday ’s rally in Tulsa , Stitt said there will be temperature checks for the attendees and he also expects most people to wear a mask .
