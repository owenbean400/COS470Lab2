President Trump said he did not get tested for coronavirus even after he met with some lawmakers who had been exposed to the virus because the White House doctor said he did not need to . 
" I do not think it 's a big deal . 
I would do it , " Trump said when asked why he had not been tested . 
He added that he feels " very good . " 
At least five members of Congress have announced that they would self quarantine after coming into contact with an individual who has been diagnosed with the novel coronavirus at the recent Conservative Political Action Conference . 
That includes Rep . 
Doug Collins , who shook Trump 's hand when the President went to Georgia on Friday to visit the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and Rep . 
Matt Gaetz , who rode with Trump in the presidential limousine and took Air Force One back to Washington with him on Monday .
