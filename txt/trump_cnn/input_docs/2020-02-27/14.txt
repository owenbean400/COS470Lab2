Vice President Mike Pence began his remarks at the Conservative Political Action Conference with reassurances about the Trump administration ’s handling of the coronavirus . 
" President Trump has no higher priority than the health safety and wellbeing of Americans , " Pence said . 
He said that the President has taken " unprecedented action " since the outbreak of the virus , touting Trump ’s decision to shut down travel from affected areas . 
Pence also touched on the newest case of coronavirus , the first in the United States contracted though community spread , painting a rosy picture by saying it was the only new case detected in the last two weeks . 
" While the risk to the American public remains low like the President said yesterday we ’re ready . 
We ’re ready for anything , " he said . 
Trump had directed him " to lead a whole of government approach " to the disease , Pence said .
