Stocks rallied Monday after the Trump administration approved a potential Covid 19 treatment . 
Wall Street finished in the green , and both the S&P 500 and the Nasdaq Composite finished at all time highs . 
The S&P ended up 1 % , finishing above 3,400 points for the first time . 
The Nasdaq closed 0.6 % higher . 
Both indexes surpassed the record levels they reached on Friday . 
The Dow closed 1.4 % , or 378 points , higher . 
Even though the index performed the best out of the three major benchmarks , it remains more than 4 % below the all time high it hit in February .
