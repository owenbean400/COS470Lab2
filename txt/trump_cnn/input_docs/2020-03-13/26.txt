President Trump said Friday he does not currently support the House Democrats ’ coronavirus bill , which would among other things provide paid leave to Americans who ca not go into work during the pandemic . 
" We thought we had something , but all of a sudden they did not agree to certain things , " Trump said of Democrats . 
House Speaker Nancy Pelosi and Treasury Secretary Steve Mnuchin spoke eight times Friday as they worked to negotiate a coronavirus response all sides can support , although sticking points over the paid leave aspects of the legislation remain . 
" They ’re not doing what ’s right for the country , " Trump said .
