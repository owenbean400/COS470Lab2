Vermont Sen . 
Bernie Sanders said he ’s organizing a letter to try and rally Democrats to " stand tall " and fight for another round of stimulus checks to be included in a coronavirus relief bill . 
Asked if any Republicans will be signing onto the letter , the senator said he hopes Republican Sen . 
Josh Hawley , who he ’s spoken to and has also said he supports direct checks , will help rally other Republicans . 
" I think Trump supporters out there like everybody else are hurting right now , " Sanders told reporters . 
" Many have lost their jobs … They want that $ 1,200 , and I hope that the Republican leadership and members of the Republican caucus understand that . "
