Democratic presidential nominee Joe Biden said President Trump and Education Secretary Betsy De Vos " have not stepped up " when it comes to safely reopening schools , and he called for " straightforward , common sense solutions . " 
" We all want our schools to reopen safely with a plan that prioritizes the health of our students and educators and staff alike , " Biden said in remarks delivered from Wilmington , Delaware . 
Biden laid out some of his school reopening plans which he said were posted on his campaign website . 
The former vice president said the Trump administration is " starving schools of the needed funding . " 
Biden also said that if elected president , he would also support additional funding for mental health in schools and funds to hire additional teachers as smaller classes mean " more educators are needed . "
