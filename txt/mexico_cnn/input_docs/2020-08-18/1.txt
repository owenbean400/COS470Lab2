Mexico 's health ministry announced what it called " good news " Tuesday morning , saying the country is " in a decreasing phase " of the coronavirus outbreak . 
On Monday , Mexico recorded its lowest number of new cases since June , adding 3,571 new Covid 19 cases . 
The rolling average of new daily cases , measured over the week since Aug . 10 , stood at 5,700 . 
The average has fallen steadily from an all time peak of 7,022 on Aug . 1 . 
However , the daily average of deaths ( measured across one week ) from coronavirus peaked only on Aug . 11 at 702 , and has fallen only slightly since then . 
Mexico has 525,733 total confirmed coronavirus cases and 57,203 deaths from the virus .
