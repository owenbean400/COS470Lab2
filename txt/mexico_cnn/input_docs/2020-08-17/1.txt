Mexico recorded 4,448 new cases of the novel coronavirus on Sunday , taking the total number of cases identified in the country to 522,162 . 
The Health Ministry also added 214 new fatalities , raising the death toll to 56,757 . 
Mexico has reported the third highest number of deaths in the world from coronavirus , following only the United States and Brazil , according to Johns Hopkins University ( JHU ) . 
Only Brazil and Peru have recorded more Covid 19 cases than Mexico in Latin America , according to JHU 's tally .
