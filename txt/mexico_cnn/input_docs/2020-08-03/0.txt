Another 4,853 people were diagnosed with Covid 19 in Mexico , the country 's Health Ministry said Sunday , bringing the nationwide total to 439,046 . 
The ministry also reported 274 new deaths , bring Mexico 's death toll to 47,746 . 
Authorities reported a record 9,556 new cases Saturday . 
On Friday , Mexico coronavirus death toll surpassed that of the United Kingdom . 
Only the US and Brazil have seen more virus related fatalities , according to Johns Hopkins University .
