Mexico recorded 6,026 new Covid 19 infections on Wednesday , taking the total number of cases confirmed in the country to 579,914 . 
The Health Ministry also recorded 518 new fatalities , bringing Mexico 's coronavirus death toll to 62,594 . 
Mexico has reported the third highest number of deaths in the world from the virus , behind only the United States and Brazil , according to Johns Hopkins University ( JHU ) In Latin America , only Brazil and Peru have recorded more Covid 19 cases than Mexico , JHU data shows .
