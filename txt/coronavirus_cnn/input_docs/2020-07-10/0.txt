Coronavirus is winning the battle in El Centro , California . 
It 's in almost every patient in the hospital . 
Tents are being put up to handle even more cases . 
It 's on the streets , forcing rescuers to put on protective suits and cumbersome masks before they approach people needing help . 
And it 's not stopping . 
Even as nearby and faraway facilities take patients to try to ease the load , more and more sick people keep coming . 
And so do the deaths . 
The workers are exhausted . 
The virus is not .
