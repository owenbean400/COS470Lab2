Covid 19 has changed the way the world looks at the importance of hand hygiene – for the better , Henrietta Fore , executive director of United Nations Children 's Fund ( UNICEF ) , said Friday . 
" How often we wash our hands , how we use soap , " she said . 
" This is not available everywhere in the world , so if we can focus on getting good … wash systems for water and soap around the developing world it will have a lasting impact and it will change both what healthcare workers can do , but also how communities can keep themselves safe . "
