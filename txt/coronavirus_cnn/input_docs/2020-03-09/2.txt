Federal regulators on Monday chastised seven companies for selling " unapproved drugs " that claim to prevent or treat coronavirus . 
These drugs " pose significant risks to patient health and violate federal law , " the regulators said . 
The companies , which sell teas , essential oils , tinctures and colloidal silver , received warning letters from the US Food and Drug Administration and the US Federal Trade Commission . 
They have 48 hours to respond , the agencies said , or risk facing product seizures and legal injunctions . 
More context : No vaccines or drugs are currently approved to treat the novel coronavirus , according to the FDA .
