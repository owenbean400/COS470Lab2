There are a lot of steps that go into containing and mitigating the novel coronavirus , also known as Covid 19 . 
The strategy may include controlling the disease by testing for the virus as early as possible , tracing the contacts of an infected person and by preventing infections in health care facilities . 
It may also include non pharmaceutical interventions for individuals and communities , like encouraging good hygiene and canceling events where a lot of people will gather .
