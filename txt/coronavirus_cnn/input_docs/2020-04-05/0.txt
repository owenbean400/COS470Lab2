Louisiana could run out of ventilators by the end of the week if the number of confirmed coronavirus cases continues to surge , Gov . 
John Bel Edwards told CNN today . 
Edwards received " an additional 200 ventilators from the federal Strategic National Stockpile " on Saturday , Edwards said . 
But looking to the future , " we definitely see that we will exceed our ventilator capacity , " he said . 
Louisiana currently has 12,496 confirmed cases and 409 deaths , according to a tally from Johns Hopkins University .
