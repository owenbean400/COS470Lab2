US stocks opened slightly lower today , ahead of what could be the beginning of a sour earnings season . 
Coronavirus could take a big bite out of earnings , even though the pandemic did not shut down the US economy until mid March . 
Last week was one for the history books for stocks , with the S&P 500 recording its best week since 1974 . 
The Dow opened 0.4 % , or 100 points , lower . 
The S&P 500 kicked off 0.4 % lower . 
The Nasdaq Composite also fell 0.4 % .
