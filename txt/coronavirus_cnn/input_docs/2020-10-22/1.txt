More than 1,600 new Covid 19 cases were reported in Oklahoma , according to the state 's Department of Health . 
The department recorded 1,628 new cases — the highest daily total of new cases since the pandemic began . 
A total of 112,483 cases have been reported in Oklahoma so far , according to the state 's dashboard . 
At least 11 new deaths were reported , bringing the total to 1,221 . 
Hospitalizations have steadily increased over the past seven days , according to the dashboard . 
The department said 991 patients with Covid 19 are currently hospitalized statewide . 
Note : These numbers were released by Oklahoma 's public health agency and may not line up exactly in real time with CNN ’s database , drawn from Johns Hopkins University and the Covid Tracking Project .
