Dr . Anthony Fauci warned against a false sense of security from the decreasing coronavirus mortality rate in the United States . 
" It ’s a false narrative to take comfort in a lower rate of death , " the nation ’s top infectious disease expert cautioned in a press conference on Tuesday with Sen . 
Doug Jones , an Alabama Democrat . 
" There ’s so many other things that are very dangerous and bad about this virus , do not get yourself into false complacency , " Fauci urged . 
Fauci , the director of the National Institutes on Allergy and Infectious Diseases , made the comment as the White House repeatedly pointed to the falling death rate from Covid 19 cases as proof the virus is under control . 
Coronavirus cases have actually surged in states across the South and Southwest and hospitals in some areas are reaching ICU capacity .
