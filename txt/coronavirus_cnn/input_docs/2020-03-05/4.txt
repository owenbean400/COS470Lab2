Talking about the coronavirus is hard enough when you 're an adult . 
The information , numbers and advice concerning the outbreak seem to shift by the day , and the abstract nature of the threat can invite plenty of fear and uncertainty . 
However , if everyone is talking about the virus , your children are definitely listening . 
Here is some expert advice on how to start this important conversation , and keep your child safe and reassured .
