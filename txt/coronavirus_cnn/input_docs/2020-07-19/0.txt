Coronavirus fatalities around Austin , Texas , rose by a third during the past two weeks , Mayor Steve Adler told CNN 's Wolf Blitzer on Saturday night . 
" The people that had gotten sicker more than three weeks ago , before we did the masking , are now passing away in my hospitals , " Adler said . 
" A third of my total deaths in my city have happened over the last two weeks . " 
Travis County , which includes the city of Austin , has now seen 63 deaths from Covid 19 since July 3 . 
The county has recorded 196 deaths from the virus since the pandemic began , according to Texas Department of State Health Services . 
Adler said Austin was an example of why wearing a mask is so important . 
Texas Governor Greg Abbott issued a mask mandate on July 2 for any county with 20 or more positive cases .
