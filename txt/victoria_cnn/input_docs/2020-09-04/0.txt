The Australian state of Victoria reported 81 fresh coronavirus cases and 59 related deaths on Friday , according to its health department . 
The relatively high number of new fatalities comes after a backlog of deaths were reported from July and August . 
" There were 81 new cases and sadly 59 deaths , " a tweet from the health department read . 
" This includes 50 people in aged care who passed away in July - August . " 
Victoria had a significant rise in cases over August , with many new infections in aged care homes and among health care workers . 
Victorian Premier Daniel Andrews announced a state of emergency on August 3 .
