Police and protesters have clashed in Santiago amid a city wide lockdown as local officials warned that food shortages have hit one of the Chilean capital ’s poorest neighbourhoods . 
Reuters reports that a group of protesters threw rocks and burned piles of wood along a street in the destitute neighbourhood on Santiago ’s southern fringe . 
Images on social media and local television showed police spraying tear gas and water cannons to disperse the growing crowd . 
The municipality said families were going hungry in the poorest sectors of El Bosque , a neighborhood where many work informally , or not at all . 
The city district has been under quarantine since mid April , city officials said .
