Brazil ’s Butantan Institute biomedical centre will receive this week the first doses of China ’s Sinovac vaccine against Covid 19 , its director , Dimas Covas , said on Tuesday , speaking remotely in a congressional hearing . 
Covas said the preliminary results of the trials Butantan is conducting in Brazil indicate the vaccine , called Corona Vac , has an excellent safety profile . 
He said Butantan expects to have 46 million doses ready for use in January .
