Registered voters in California will receive mail in ballots for the November election , state governor Gavin Newsom has said . 
He added that the state was also working to set up safe places for in person voting . 
California ’s secretary of state , Alex Padilla , who joined Newsom by phone during the state ’s daily coronavirus briefing , said California was the first state to send every voter a ballot in advance of the election . 
Return postage will be prepaid . 
Concerns have been growing about the effect of coronavirus on in person voting .
