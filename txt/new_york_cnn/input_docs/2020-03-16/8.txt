New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo , who is working with with the governors of New Jersey and Connecticut , announced a number of business closures earlier this morning . 
Bars and restaurants close at 8 p.m . ET . 
All local governments must reduce their workforce by 50 % minimum and all non essential personnel can stay home . 
Meanwhile , Cuomo outlined the businesses that are considered essential and exempt from the shutdown : Grocery stores Gas stations Pharmacies Medical facilities
