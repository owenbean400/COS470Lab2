New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo stressed the importance of federal assistance during the coronavirus outbreak . 
He said the state needs to find facilities to be converted to hospitals and he hopes to not pay money to acquire new land . 
While noting that New York state has built bridges and other infrastructure , Cuomo said " We do not have billions of dollars you would need . " 
" This state ca not do it , no state can do it , " he added . 
Cuomo is asking local governments to " immediately identify a number of beds in facilities that are available . "
