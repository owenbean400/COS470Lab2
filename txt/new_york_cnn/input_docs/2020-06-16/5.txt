While some other states have seen an upward trend in hospitalizations , New York state reported its lowest number of Covid 19 hospitalizations since the pandemic started in the state , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said in a news conference today . 
Total Covid 19 hospitalizations fell to 1,538 yesterday , the lowest since March 20 , Cuomo tweeted . 
He also said that the three day rolling average of Covid 19 fatalities is also at a a new record low at 24 . 
The state reported 25 deaths on June 15 .
