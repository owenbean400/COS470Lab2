New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said hospitals across the state can now allow visitors as coronavirus numbers continue to improve . 
" The numbers look very good , " he said at a news conference . 
" We 're going to allow hospitals to accept visitors at their discretion . " 
Cuomo said that if a hospital chooses to allow visitors , those guests must follow state guidelines , including wearing personal protective equipment and being subject to symptom checks . 
The visits may be time limited . 
Cuomo also added that group homes can allow visitors starting on Friday , if they follow similar guidelines .
