To alleviate some of the financial hardships New Yorkers are experiencing due to the spread of coronavirus , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo announced a 90 day mortgage relief for homeowners across the state . 
According to Cuomo , this mortgage relief includes : Waive mortgage payments based on financial hardship No negative reporting to credit bureaus Grace period for load modification No late payment fees or online payment fees Postponing or suspending foreclosures
