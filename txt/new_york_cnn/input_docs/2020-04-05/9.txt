In a haunting letter to his friends and colleagues , a Columbia University surgeon describes how coronavirus has forced doctors to ration care for very sick patients who do not have the virus , but still need medical procedures . 
Bacha explains that nurses have been deployed to care for coronavirus patients , and that safety gear is scarce . 
" The lack of Personal Protective Equipment is galling . 
Who would have thought that a simple plain surgical mask , something we use and discard multiple times a day during normal times , would become a rationed product in one of the richest cities in the world ? " he wrote . 
He said his pediatric heart surgery program was down to one operating team , " barely . "
