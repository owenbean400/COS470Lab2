Connecticut Gov . 
Ned Lamont said today that the national strategic stockpile is empty . 
Lamont said the state has received 50 ventilators from the federal government , but said , " Now , we are on our own . " 
Lamont said Connecticut still needs more personal protective equipment and supplies , saying he felt like a general sending soldiers into battle without proper protection . 
Lamont also said that Connecticut is the fourth most Covid 19 infected state in the country , per capita , behind New York , New Jersey and Louisiana . 
He urged everyone to think of the Covid 19 epicenter as a region , the New York Metro region , instead of state by state .
