The United States now has at least 1,528,661 cases of coronavirus and 91,938 related deaths , according to Johns Hopkins University . 
The totals includes cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases . 
New York state remains the hardest hit , with 352,845 cases and 28,558 deaths -- that 's 147 deaths per 100,000 residents , the highest rate nationwide . 
New Jersey , Illinois , and Massachusetts follow next , in that order .
