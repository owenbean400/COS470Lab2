The United States recorded 57,540 new coronavirus cases and 1,399 virus related deaths on Tuesday , according to data from Johns Hopkins University . 
That raises the national total to at least 4,771,080 infections and 156,801 fatalities . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases . 
California remains the state with the highest number of cases , at 526,685 . 
Florida follows , then Texas , New York , and Georgia . 
But New York still has the highest number of Covid deaths , at 32,725 . 
Only one other state -- neighboring New Jersey -- has crossed the 10,000 mark in its death toll .
