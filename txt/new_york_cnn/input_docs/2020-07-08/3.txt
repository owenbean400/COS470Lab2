New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo today laid out the timeline for how the state will make the decisions about reopening schools in the fall . 
School districts will submit their plans for reopening by the end of the month , the governor said during a coronavirus briefing today . 
After that , Cuomo said , the state will " look at the data in that first week " of August and " we 'll make a decision " on reopening schools . 
Cuomo said that the state " will open the schools if it 's safe . "
