New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said at a news conference today that the state needs federal assistance to restart the economy . 
He said , " that is going to be a federal stimulus bill , " adding , " there 's no other way to do this . " 
Cuomo repeated his criticism of the stimulus bill that was recently passed by Congress , calling it " woefully inadequate " for New York . 
He said that he will be sending the state 's congressional delegation a letter today telling them " the past legislation did good for the nation ... but it was not fair to New York . " 
He added " that has to be remedied " in any legislation going forward .
