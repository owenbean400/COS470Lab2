New data shows nearly 70 % of the hotel rooms in the United States were vacant last week , according to data and analytics group STR . 
That national occupancy rate is a 56 % decline from the same week last year . 
The average occupied room cost is about $ 93 , also down from last year . 
The San Francisco area and New York recorded the worst declines , with less than 17 % of hotel rooms in both cities occupied , according to STR . 
That represents an 80 % decrease from this time last year . 
Many hotels across the country have closed entirely . 
One example , The Boston Harbor Hotel , a luxury property in downtown Boston , closed last Saturday , according to its website , and hopes to reopen May 18 .
