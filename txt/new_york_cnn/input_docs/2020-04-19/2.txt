Speaking today at the Feinstein Institute for Medical Research in Manhasset , New York , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo shared Sunday that hospitalizations across the state are on the decline . 
Cuomo added : " If the data holds and if this trend holds , we are past the high point and all indications at this point is that we are on the descent . 
Whether or not the descent continues depends on what we do but right now we 're on the descent . "
