The state of New York has more cases than any other country in the world , according to a tally by Johns Hopkins University . 
New York is reporting 161,807 coronavirus cases according to a tally by JHU . 
Spain ( No . 2 ) , the country with the second most coronavirus cases behind the US , has 157,022 cases . 
Italy ( No . 3 ) is reporting 143,626 cases . 
France ( No . 4 ) and Germany ( No . 5 ) are both reporting more than 118,000 cases , according to tallies by JHU .
