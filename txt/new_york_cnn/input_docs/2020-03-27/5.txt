US Surgeon General Dr . Jerome Adams said Friday on " CBS This Morning " that the Federal Emergency Management Agency has sent a team to New York City to help allocate resources . 
" You heard Governor Cuomo say , look , we have actually resources , they 're just mismatched , " Adams told CBS ' Gayle King . 
" So we sent a team , a FEMA team to help New York City to make sure the resources are getting to where they need . "
