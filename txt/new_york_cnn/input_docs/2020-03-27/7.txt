New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said the state is looking at " about 21 days for a possible apex " for the peak number of coronavirus cases . 
He continued : " So we want to do everything we can to be ready for that increased capacity that could hit us in 21 days and ramp up the hospital capacity . " 
He said that , at that time , New York will need 140,000 hospital beds . 
There are currently 53,000 beds . 
The press conference is ongoing .
