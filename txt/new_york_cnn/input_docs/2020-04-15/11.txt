New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said he is issuing an executive order that all people in the state must wear a mask or mouth and nose covering in public , whenever they are in a situation where they cannot maintain social distancing of 6 feet or less . 
There will be a three day notice period before the order is enforced , allowing New Yorkers to secure masks or face coverings , Cuomo announced Wednesday afternoon . 
Local governments should start to enforce it , but there wo not be any penalties as of right now , he added .
