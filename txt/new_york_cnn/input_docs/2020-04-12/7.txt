Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo is signing an executive order to expand the number of people who are eligible to conduct the coronavirus antibody test . 
This test will determine if someone had and got over the virus , Cuomo said , and would determine if the person theoretically has immunity to the virus for an undetermined period of time . 
The governor is also signing an executive order directing all employers to provide essential workers with a cloth or surgical face mask when they are interacting with the public . 
The masks should be provided cost free , Cuomo said .
