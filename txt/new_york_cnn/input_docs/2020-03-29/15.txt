President Donald Trump announced a new initiative Sunday called an " air bridge " to help bring supplies to New York . 
The President said that a flight arrived this morning at New York 's John F . Kennedy International Airport with 2 million masks and gowns , more than 10 million gloves and 70,000 thermometers . 
He said that currently they are expecting 51 flights with supplies from other countries , though there will be " intercountry things " as well .
