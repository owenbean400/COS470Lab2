New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said during his daily press conference at least 8,503 people are in the hospital for coronavirus . 
At least 965 people have died from coronavirus across New York , he said . 
That number is up from 728 previously reported . 
He also said at least 282 people are in intensive care across New York and about 165 people have been intubated . 
There are 59,313 confirmed Covid 19 cases across New York , Cuomo said . 
At least 172,360 people have been tested .
