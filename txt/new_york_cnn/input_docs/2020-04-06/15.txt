New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo says he wants local law enforcement to demonstrate " more aggressive " enforcement of social distancing . 
He said he is increasing the fine of violating state directives from $ 500 to $ 1,000 . 
He added that individuals " do not have the right " to take the lives of health care staff and people who are " literally putting their lives on the line " and be " cavalier or reckless with them . "
