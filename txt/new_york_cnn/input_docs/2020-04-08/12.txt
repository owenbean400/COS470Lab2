New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo pointed out that the death toll in the state so far during the coronavirus crisis has far eclipsed the number of people killed during the September 11 , 2001 , attacks on the World Trade Center . 
Cuomo said that the 2,753 New Yorkers were killed at the World Trade Center , while 6,268 have died so far in the coronavirus pandemic .
