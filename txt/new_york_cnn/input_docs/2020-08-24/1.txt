New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo announced that the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey and NY Health and Hospitals will be setting up new Covid 19 testing sites at John F . Kennedy and La Guardia airports in New York City for incoming passengers . 
He did not elaborate on the details . 
This decision comes as New York continues to enforce its travel advisory which requires a 14 day quarantine for individuals traveling in from Covid 19 hot spots .
