New York , New Jersey and Connecticut will require people traveling from states that have a high infection rate quarantine for 14 days . 
New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo announced the travel advisory today alongside the governors of New Jersey and Connecticut . 
He added : " It would not be malicious or malevolent , but it would still be real , so we are jointly instituting the travel advisory today because what happens in New York happens in New Jersey happens in Connecticut . "
