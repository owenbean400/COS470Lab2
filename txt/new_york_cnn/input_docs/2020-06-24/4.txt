Following his announcement that New York will institute a 14 day quarantine alongside New Jersey and Connecticut for travelers from states with high Covid 19 rates , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said the state 's latest coronavirus deaths are about " as low as it 's ever been " and he called the figures " great news . " 
The state reported 17 deaths yesterday , down from 27 reported on Monday . 
The governor stressed that the state does not want to see these " numbers reverse . " 
He added that the state 's approach to reopening following data " has been vindicated . "
