New York Mayor Bill de Blasio said the city is within parameters regarding its Covid 19 data to proceed as planned with their phase one reopening on Monday . 
The statewide thresholds to enter phase one include : Having less than 200 people admitted to hospitals per day Having under 375 intensive care unit patients across the city Having less than 15 % of city residents testing positive for Covid 19 As of Sunday , NYC hospitals have admitted 72 people due to Covid 19 , 324 people remain in ICUs , and 4 % of the city is currently testing positive for Covid 19 , de Blasio said .
