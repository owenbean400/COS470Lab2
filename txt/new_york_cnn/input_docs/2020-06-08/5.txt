New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said today marks 100 days since the first known coronavirus case in the state . 
Cuomo said that at the start of the pandemic in the state , experts said how the virus progressed would largely depend on what residents did to stop the spread . 
He applauded New Yorkers ' efforts to flatten the curve . 
" When things are tough , New Yorkers are tougher , " Cuomo said .
