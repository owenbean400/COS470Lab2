Andrew Cuomo said the number of ICU cases and intubations in the state has increased while New York continues to grapple with the coronavirus pandemic . 
At the same time , Cuomo said the number of patients being discharged also increased . 
There are 15,905 people in the hospital and 4,126 in ICU , Cuomo said . 
As of today , 3,565 people have died due to coronavirus in New York .
