New York state reported its lowest daily coronavirus death toll so far , with 49 deaths reported yesterday , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said . 
That 's down from 58 deaths on Monday and 54 on Tuesday , the governor said . 
Hospitalizations are also at an all time low . 
Cuomo warned that although many headlines are focusing on the nationwide protests over George Floyd 's death at the hands of police , coronavirus remains a threat . 
" It is still in people and in society . 
We 're still battling that , " he said of the virus .
