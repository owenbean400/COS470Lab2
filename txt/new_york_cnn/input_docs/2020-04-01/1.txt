More than 262 million Americans are currently under stay at home orders -- over 80 % of the US population -- according to a CNN count . 
This count includes state , city and county orders . 
The numbers were tallied using US census data . 
Among the most heavily impacted states are : California : 39.5 million people New York : 19.4 million people Illinois : 12.6 million people Ohio : 11.6 million people North Carolina : 10.4 million people
