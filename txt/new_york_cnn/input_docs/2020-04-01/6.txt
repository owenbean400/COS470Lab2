Northwell Health , the largest health care system in the state of New York , told CNN that about 470 employees have tested positive for Covid 19 . 
Northwell Health spokesperson Terence Lynam said the hospital system has had more than 2,400 Covid 19 patients in their hospitals , with an additional 3,600 hospitalized patients who were either waiting to be tested or waiting for results . 
ICUs are currently 85 % full . 
The company employs about 72,000 people .
