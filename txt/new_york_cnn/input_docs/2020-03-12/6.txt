Broadway theaters in New York City are required to have maximum capacity of 50 % of total seating as of 5 p.m . ET today , New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo announced . 
" Fifty percent of your seated capacity will be the new capacity , " Cuomo said . 
As of 5 p.m . Friday , other facilities will be required to adhere to the same rules if the venue can hold up to 500 people , the governor said . 
The exceptions for that rule are : Schools Hospitals Nursing homes Mass transit All gatherings of 500 people are also banned , Cuomo said .
