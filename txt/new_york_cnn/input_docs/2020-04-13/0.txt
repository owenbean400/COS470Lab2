A rise of at least 5,695 cases on Sunday has put New York City 's total number of coronavirus infections at over 104,000 , with at least 6,182 fatalities . 
More than 189,000 cases have been reported in New York state -- the most heavily affected state in the US . 
Hundreds of people are currently dying every day from coronavirus related complications . 
Speaking on Sunday , Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said authorities wanted to reopen the state as soon as possible but said it was hard to know how the virus would continue to develop .
