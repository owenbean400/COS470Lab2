New York Mayor Bill de Blasio said his city is " ready to respond " as a first coronavirus case was identified in Manhattan . 
De Blasio tweeted the announcement Sunday , shortly after New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo announced that New York had identified its first novel coronavirus patient . 
The patient is a woman in her late 30s , who contracted the virus while traveling in Iran . 
She is currently isolated in her Manhattan home , according to the New York State Department of Health .
