New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo says the state is in dire need of ventilators to battle coronavirus , calling ventilators " the key piece of equipment . " 
" If you are a regulated health facility , we are asking you by order of the Department of Health to make that ventilator available . 
We will purchase it from you . 
You could lend it to us . 
But we need ventilators , " Cuomo said . 
He said that anyone in possession of ventilators that can be sold or loaned to New York should contact the state Department of Health . 
" We need those ventilators . 
The ventilators are to this war what missiles were to World War II . "
