Italy has registered 953 new Covid 19 cases in the past 24 hours , according to the latest data from the Ministry of Civil protection . 
In the past week Italy has seen an increase of over 6000 cases , the highest since the period of May 11-17 . 
The country has had a total of 260,298 cases and 35,441 deaths . 
The Italian intensive care units are currently treating 65 Covid 19 patients , 6 less than yesterday .
