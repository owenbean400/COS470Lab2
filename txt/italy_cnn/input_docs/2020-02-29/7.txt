An Icelandic man in his 40s who recently traveled to Italy has been infected with the coronavirus , according to Icelandic authorities . 
The man traveled to Northern Italy -- where most of the coronavirus cases are centered -- but he did not go to what authorities have termed the " designated risk zone , " according to Iceland ’s Department of Civil Protection and Emergency Management . 
He 's now in isolation at Landspitali National University Hospital in the country 's capital , Reykjavik . 
He is not considered severely ill but is showing common symptoms of the virus . 
The government is working to trace the man ’s path to see who else may be at risk of exposure .
