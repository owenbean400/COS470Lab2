The number of coronavirus cases in Italy climbed by 307 on Saturday , with 1,128 diagnoses of the virus confirmed , civil protection officials say . 
Fifty two percent of the cases are in house isolation , said the head of the Civil Protection Department , Angelo Borrelli . 
Another 101 people are in intensive care units , and 401 people are hospitalized with mild symptoms . 
A total of 18,500 samples have been taken in Italy . 
Most of the swabs -- 58 % -- were carried out in Lombardy , the region with the largest coronavirus cluster . 
Of the eight dead on Saturday , all were elderly -- six were in Lombardy and two in Emilia Romagna . 
Twenty nine people in all have died in Italy .
