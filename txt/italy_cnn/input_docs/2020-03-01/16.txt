The Czech Republic confirmed its first cases of the novel coronavirus , Czech Health Minister Adam Vojtech said Sunday . 
Vojtech said the three cases diagnosed included an American student studying in Milan , Italy , who had traveled to Prague as a tourist . 
In all three cases , patients had visited northern Italy . 
The cases were confirmed by the National Reference Laboratory of the Center of Toxicology and Health Security . 
The minister said laboratory samples had been sent to Berlin for further confirmation .
