Italy recorded 516 new coronavirus infections on Friday , bringing the total number of cases to 46,175 , according to the country 's Civil Protection Agency . 
However , officials say the number of active Covid 19 infections in the country continues to drop . 
There was an increase of 87 new deaths , which is in line with daily death tolls of the last few days , authorities said . 
The total number of fatalities currently stands at 33,229 . 
Over the last day , 2,240 more people recovered from the virus , bringing the total number of recoveries to 152,844 . 
There were 475 people still in intensive care on Friday , 14 fewer than on Thursday , according to authorities .
