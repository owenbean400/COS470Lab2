At least 27,682 people with Covid 19 have now died in Italy since the beginning of the crisis , data from the Italian Civil Protection Agency showed Wednesday . 
The number of active cases in the country stands at 104,657 . 
The total number of cases in Italy , including deaths and recoveries , is now 203,591 . 
More than 20,000 health workers have been infected with coronavirus , according to the National Institute for Health . 
At least 153 doctors have died of coronavirus , according to the Association of Doctors .
