Lufthansa has canceled 7,100 European flights for March because of the spread of coronavirus and the impact it 's having on demand for flights . 
The German airline says most of these canceled flights will be internal German flights and trips to Italy , accounting for around 25 % of its total capacity . 
Lufthansa is also canceling all flights to Israel because of new entry regulations . 
It says the rules will lead to a considerable drop in demand for flights to Tel Aviv and Eilat . 
Earlier this week Lufthansa said it would ground 150 aircraft because of coronavirus , out of a total fleet of 770 aircraft .
