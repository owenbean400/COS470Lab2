Greece has announced another extension of the coronavirus lockdown on its migrant camps , hours after 2,000 people protested in central Athens to mark World Refugee Day and denounce the government ’s treatment of migrants , according to Agence France Presse . 
The migration ministry said confinement for residents of reception and identification centres across the country would be extended to 5 July . 
It was due to have ended on Monday . 
Greece was quick to introduce strict confinement measures on migrant camps on 21 March and imposed a more general lockdown on 23 March . 
Rights groups have expressed concern that migrants ’ rights have been eroded by the restrictions .
