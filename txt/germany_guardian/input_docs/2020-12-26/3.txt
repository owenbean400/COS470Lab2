A black doctor who died are contracting Covid 19 described racist medical care in widely shared social media posts days before her death , prompting a hospital system in the US to promise a " full external review " into her treatment . 
In the past year , public health experts nationwide have acknowledged the role racism plays in healthcare with dozens of cities , counties and states declaring racism a public health threat . 
While public health experts have called it a first step , many are sceptical .
