Texas Gov . 
Greg Abbott said in a press conference today , " With heavy heart , I share with you that we have now lost 41 Texans to fatality connected with Covid 19 . " 
Gov . 
Abbott said that as of noon local time , 3,266 Texans have tested positive for coronavirus of the 42,922 who have taken the test , which he noted was " less than 10 % . " 
He added that 2.4 % of the total hospital beds across Texas specifically delegated to Covid 19 were occupied , while the large remainder are vacant .
