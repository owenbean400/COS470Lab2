Texas is waiving some state trucking regulations in an effort to keep grocery stores stocked , according to a news release from Gov . 
Greg Abbott . 
Following Friday 's state of disaster declaration , Abbott issued waivers to the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles allowing oversized commercial vehicles on roads and suspending several other " DMV safety limitations , " the release said . 
Abbott added : " Suspending these state trucking regulations will improve our ability to deliver the necessary supplies throughout the state so that grocers and retailers are able to continually stock their shelves . 
I want to remind Texans that stockpiling resources is neither necessary nor productive . "
