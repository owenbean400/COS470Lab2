A 37 years old man is the first inmate in the Texas prison system to test positive for coronavirus , according to the Texas Department of Criminal Justice . 
The inmate is currently in isolation at a Galveston , Texas hospital , according to department spokesperson Jeremy Desel . 
The inmate had been assigned to the Lychner State Jail when he reported a cough and shortness of breath three days ago . There are currently 140,500 inmates in the Texas prison system . 
Previously , the department had a prison employee diagnosed as positive .
