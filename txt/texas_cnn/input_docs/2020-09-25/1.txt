California is now the first US state to surpass 800,000 cases of Covid 19 , according top data from Johns Hopkins University . 
At least 15,405 people have died . 
The most populous state in the nation has now recorded more than 800,273 infections . 
Texas , the second most populous state , has identified 747,366 cases and at least 15,510 deaths . 
The third , Florida , has identified 693,040 cases and at least 13,795 virus related deaths . 
California became the first state to surpass 700,000 cases on August 29 .
