The city of El Paso , Texas , is reporting a new record high daily number of Covid 19 cases , just a day after setting the record for single day cases . 
El Paso reported 838 new cases on Friday . 
The day before they had set a daily record with 717 new cases . 
There are now 7,399 active cases in the city , according to the city 's public health department . 
So far , there have been no new deaths reported Friday . 
The city reported 423 hospitalizations as a result of Covid 19 with 112 people in the intensive care unit . 
On Thursday , the Texas Department of State Health Services said only 8 ICU beds were available in Region I , which includes El Paso .
