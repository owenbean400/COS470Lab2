New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said any state that has a 10 people per 100,000 testing positive for coronavirus — or a 10 % infection rate — will be subject to the newly imposed quarantine . 
The numbers , which are on a seven day rolling average , will be updated daily , so the states impacted could change day to day . 
Today , there are nine states that meet the criteria for the 14 day quarantine .
