Major League Baseball pitcher Brett Martin of the Texas Rangers has tested positive for Covid 19 , the team confirmed to CNN on Friday . 
The team says Martin is now in quarantine in Texas . 
Brett Martin is also diabetic . 
He was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes at the age of 16 . 
The Major League Baseball season resumed on Wednesday with players reporting to training and games are scheduled to start on July 23 or 24 .
