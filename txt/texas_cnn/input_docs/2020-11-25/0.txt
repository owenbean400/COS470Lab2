Dr . Joseph Varon , chief medical officer at Houston ’s United Memorial Medical Center , says he expects an influx of sick patients after Thanksgiving . 
Today is Dr . Varon ’s 251st continuous day of working due to the coronavirus pandemic . 
He said that he is " running out of fumes " but he " ca not get a day off , because nobody cares for the patients that I can care for . " 
He said that he sees people who still do not listen to guidelines . 
" You go outside , and it 's like nothing has happened . 
People are out there , restaurants , bars , I mean , just name it , " he said .
