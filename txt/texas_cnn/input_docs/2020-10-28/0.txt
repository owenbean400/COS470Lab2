Los Angeles Dodgers third baseman Justin Turner tested positive for Covid 19 , MLB commissioner Rob Manfred said after the Dodgers won the World Series in Arlington , Texas on Tuesday . 
Turner was removed during the game . 
In a tweet , Turner said he felt " great " and had " no symptoms . " 
" Thanks to everyone reaching out ! " 
Turner said . 
" I feel great , no symptoms at all . 
Just experienced every emotion you can possibly imagine . 
Ca not believe I could not be out there to celebrate with my guys ! 
So proud of this team & unbelievably happy for the City of LA " However , Turner later returned to the field to celebrate with his teammates and the World Series trophy . 
This was shown on FOX Sports 1 in a World Series postgame show moments ago . 
The Dodgers defeated the Tampa Bay Rays 3-1 in Game 6 to win the best of seven series four games to two , ending a 32 year wait to be crowned champions .
