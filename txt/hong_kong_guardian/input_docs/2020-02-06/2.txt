An investigation at Altnagelvin Hospital in Derry , Northern Ireland , is under way tonight over a suspected case of coronavirus involving a mother and baby . 
It is understood that the mother and child recently returned to the city from Hong Kong and reported to Derry ’s main hospital A&amp ; E department earlier today . 
Northern Ireland ’s Public Health Agency said they " are not commenting on individual cases at the moment " . 
A spokesperson for the PHA said no cases had yet been diagnosed in Northern Ireland .
