Hong Kong health authorities are reportedly about to announce five new recorded cases - making it two weeks since the city reported figures above 20 . 
The South China Morning Post also reported one of the five cases is a 22 year old student at the Hong Kong Institute of Vocational Education Lee Wai Lee campus in Tseung Kwan O , where more than 2,000 other students and staff were being now tested . 
On Saturday the overall number of local cases stood at 5,108 , with 105 related deaths .
