Reuters is reporting that El Salvador ’s president , Nayib Bukele , has accused Mexico of allowing a dozen confirmed coronavirus cases to board a flight due to leave Mexico City for San Salvador He said he ’s suspended all passenger air travel with immediate effect . 
Bukele , who made the assertion on Twitter , did not provide evidence or more detail about the situation . 
Mexican authorities , which Bukele described as " irresponsible , " did not immediately respond to Reuters ’ request for comment .
