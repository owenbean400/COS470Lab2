Serbia is to start tests of Russia ’s Sputnik V vaccine , as it continues talks with Pfizer about purchasing the jab . 
Laboratories will get 20 doses this week for testing , according to a statement from the country ’s prime minister , Ana Brnabić . 
Russia has said interim trial results show the vaccine is 92 % effective at protecting people from Covid 19 . 
Before Serbia adopts any vaccine , it must be approved by its medicines and medical devices agency . 
So far , 1,604 people have died amid 175,438 cases in the Balkan country .
