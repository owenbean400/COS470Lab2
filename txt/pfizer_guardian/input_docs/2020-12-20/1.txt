Qatar is the latest country to approve the Pfizer vaccine for emergency use , Qatar state news agency , QNA , announced on Twitter . 
Qatar is due to receive its first shipment of the vaccine on Monday . 
In implementation of directives of HH the Amir regarding the arrival of the first shipment of the COVID 19 vaccine produced by Pfizer and Bio NTech on Monday , and the directives of HE Prime Minister and Minister of Interior , MOPH issued approval and emergency use of the vaccine .
