Bloomberg News is reporting - and the New York Times ’ Maggie Haberman says she has separately confirmed - that top Trump aide Nick Luna has tested positive for Covid 19 . 
Luna serves as Trump ’s " body man " , a personal attendant to the president , and was diagnosed a little over 24 hours after Trump ’s admission to Walter Reed hospital , the report says . 
Haberman reports Luna is married to Cassidy Dumbauld , a senior advisor of Jared Kushner , husband of Trump ’s daughter , Ivanka .
