Donald Trump has said the federal government will not be extending its coronavirus social distancing guidelines once they expire on Thursday , even as the number of Americans who have died of coronavirus surpassed 60,000 . 
The country has recorded 60,207 deaths from coronavirus , and 1,030,487 cases of the virus have been confirmed in the US according to data compiled by Johns Hopkins University . 
The US accounts for around one third of all confirmed cases worldwide .
