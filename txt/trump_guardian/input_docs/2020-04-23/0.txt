President Trump has begun his daily briefing . 
He has been joined today by Bill Bryan , who heads the science and technology directorate at the Department of Homeland Security . 
Bryan is presenting more information on how the virus reacts to heat , humidity and light . 
He says the virus survives best indoors , in dry conditions . 
UV rays and hotter , more humid temperatures seem to cut down the half life of the virus . 
He is at pains to say the research is ongoing . 
" Every week or two weeks we are finding out something different , " he says , acknowledging the ongoing nature of the fight against the virus .
