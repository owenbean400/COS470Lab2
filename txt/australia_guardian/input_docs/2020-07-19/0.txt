There have been several reports about the " long tail " of coronavirus , sufferers with symptoms and fatigue many weeks after initially contracting the disease . 
In this piece , produced by our Australian office , the writer Anna Poletti tells of her experience . 
" We are four months into the pandemic and we are all tired and some people are wondering , because they lack direct firsthand experience of anyone with the virus , whether there is really anything to worry about . 
I caught the virus very early , and I ca not tell you how long it takes to recover from it , because I have not recovered . "
