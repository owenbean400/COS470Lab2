On Saturday night , former first ladies Laura Bush and Michelle Obama appeared on the Global Citizen " One World : Together At Home " televised concert from their own homes , sharing messages of thanks and hope . 
" Laura and I want to express our overwhelming gratitude to the medical professionals , first responders and so many others on the front lines risking their lives on our behalf , " said Obama . 
" And we 're thankful for our pharmacists , the veterinarians , the police officers , the sanitation workers , and those of you working in grocery stores or delivering food or supplies to our homes . 
You 're the fabric of our country and your strength will carry us through this crisis , " said Bush . 
Obama finished the message off by saying : The event was organized to raise funds for the Covid 19 Solidarity Response Fund for the World Health Organization .
