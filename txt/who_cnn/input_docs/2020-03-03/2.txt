Carnival Cruise Lines is changing some ship itineraries as some countries are not allowing ships to dock because of concerns over coronavirus . 
Carnival said in a statement it is changing itineraries for ships scheduled to sail to Grand Cayman and Jamaica this week . 
" A number of Caribbean destinations continue to work through their policies with regards to cruise ship visits . 
And while we are following all US CDC and World Health Organization screening protocols and guidelines , we want to avoid any possibility of a visit to a destination where there is uncertainty or we risk being turned away , " the cruise line said in a statement .
