There were 294,237 new Covid 19 cases reported to the World Health Organization in the last 24 hours , according to a situation report published Saturday . 
The total number of cases that have been reported to WHO from around the globe is now 21 million . 
The rise in newly reported cases sets another record for cases reported to WHO within a 24 hour period . 
The previous record was set July 31 when 292,527 new cases of Covid 19 were reported . 
Saturday 's report noted there were 9,985 additional Covid 19 deaths reported to WHO in the past 24 hours , bringing the total number of deaths worldwide to 755,786 .
