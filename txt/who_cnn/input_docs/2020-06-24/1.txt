The World Health Organization expects the world will reach 10 million coronavirus cases within the next week . 
" More than 9.1 million cases of Covid 19 have now been reported to WHO , and more than 470,000 deaths . 
In the first month of this outbreak , less than 10,000 cases were reported to WHO . 
In the last month , almost 4 million cases have been reported , " WHO Director General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus said during a briefing Wednesday . 
He continued : " We expect to reach a total of 10 million cases within the next week . 
This is a sober reminder that even as we continue research into vaccines and therapeutics , we have an urgent responsibility to do everything we can with the tools we have now to suppress transmission and save lives . "
