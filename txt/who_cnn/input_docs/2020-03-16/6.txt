The World Health Organization has shipped almost 1.5 million novel coronavirus tests to 120 countries , WHO Director General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus said during a news briefing on Monday . 
He continued : " The most effective way to prevent infections and save lives is breaking the chains of transmission , and to do that you must test and isolate . 
You cannot fight a fire blindfolded and we cannot stop this pandemic if we do not know who is infected . 
We have a simple message for all countries : test , test , test . "
