The coronavirus pandemic is not over , World Health Organization director general Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus said . 
He said the WHO " continues to be concerned about the increasing trends in Africa , Eastern Europe , Latin America and some Asian countries . " 
" As in all regions , cases and deaths are underreported in many countries in these regions , because of low testing capacity , " Tedros said . 
" This virus will not be defeated if we are not united , if we are not united , the virus will exploit the cracks between us and continue to create havoc . 
Lives will be lost , " Tedros added .
