Singapore confirmed 47 new coronavirus cases yesterday -- its largest single day increase in cases so far . 
Some 33 of the new cases were imported from Europe , North America , and Southeast Asia -- all regions that are experiencing spikes in cases , said the Singapore Ministry of Health in a statement . 
Of the remaining cases , nine are linked to previous confirmed cases in the city , and five have no such associations . 
These 47 cases raise the national total to 313 . 
A total of 117 patients have fully recovered and been discharged from hospital . 
Some 196 patients are still being treated in hospital , including 15 who are in critical condition . 
New measures : The jump in cases comes soon after Singapore announced that all travelers arriving in the country will face a mandatory 14 day home quarantine , effective Saturday . 
Singapore also advised all its citizens not to travel abroad .
