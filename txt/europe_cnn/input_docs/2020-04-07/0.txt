Ninety four doctors have died of coronavirus , the Italian association of doctors said Tuesday . 
Twenty six nurses have also died after contracting the virus , the press officer of the Italian federation of nurses said , adding that there were 6,549 infected nurses in total . 
As of Monday , the total number of healthcare workers infected by coronavirus is 12,681 , according to the Italian Institute of Health . 
Protective equipment shortage : A lack of proper protective gear has made contagion inside healthcare facilities a contributor to the rampant spread of the virus . 
Some of the best health structures in Europe are in northern Italy , but they were pushed to near collapse with the sheer number of Covid 19 patients in need of urgent care .
