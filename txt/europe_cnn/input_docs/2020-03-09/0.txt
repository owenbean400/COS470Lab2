The second presumptive positive case of coronavirus has been identified in Minnesota , according to a press release from the state Department of Health . 
The case is a resident of Carver County in their 50s who was likely exposed to the virus while traveling in Europe in late February , the release said . 
The patient developed symptoms on March 2 and sought health care Saturday , according to the release . 
The patient is in isolation at home and is recovering , the release said . 
Health officials are working to identify and contact anyone who was in contact with the patient . 
Anyone identified will be asked to quarantine for 14 days , according to the release .
