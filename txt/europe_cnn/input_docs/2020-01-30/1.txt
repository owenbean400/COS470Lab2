As Americans digest the news that the first US case of person to person transmission of the Wuhan virus has been confirmed , a reminder that authorities believe there was a case of person to person transmission in Germany several days ago . Health experts believe the first such case in Europe happened last week , when a 33 year old German man contracted the virus in a business meeting with a Chinese national . 
The Chinese staff member is originally from Shanghai and has since flown back to China . 
She felt ill on the flight back and tested positive for the coronavirus after her return to Shanghai . 
The case was significant because most people diagnosed with the virus outside of China have recently travelled to the country . 
However , the German man who fell ill did not .
