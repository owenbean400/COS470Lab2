An Italian citizen has tested positive for coronavirus . 
The 29 year old man was among the 56 Italians repatriated from Wuhan on Monday and quarantined at a military facility near Rome . 
Italy ’s higher health council confirmed that the man had tested positive for the virus and is being treated at Rome ’s Lazzaro Spallanzani institute for infectious diseases . 
This is the third confirmed coronavirus case in Italy . 
A Chinese couple , who arrived in Italy on 23 January , are also being treated at the Spallanzani hospital .
