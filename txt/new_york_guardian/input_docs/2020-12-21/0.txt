Delta Air Lines confirmed on Monday it will require pre departure Covid 19 tests before passengers leave from the UK to New York . 
The New York governor , Andrew Cuomo , first disclosed the decision on Twitter after British Airways earlier said it would take the step as international leaders reacted to news of a highly infectious new strain . 
A Delta spokeswoman confirmed the decision to Reuters . 
NEW : @ Delta has also agreed to require pre departure COVID tests before passengers leave from the U.K . to New York , joining @ British Airways . 
Thank you , Delta .
