A 69 year old Italian man is the sixth person to be confirmed infected with the novel coronavirus in India . 
The new case was confirmed by Dr . Sudhir Bhandari of SMS Hospital in Jaipur . 
The patient is being kept in isolation at the hospital . 
Bhandari says the hospital is in touch with the Italian embassy in New Delhi and is trying to track down 18 other Italian citizens who were traveling with the man .
