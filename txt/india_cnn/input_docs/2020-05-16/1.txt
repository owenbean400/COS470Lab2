At least 24 migrant workers were killed and dozens injured in India when a truck they were traveling in collided with another vehicle early Saturday , police said . 
The incident took place in Auraiya district , in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh , police official Suresh Kumar told CNN . 
An investigation is underway . 
Thousands of migrant workers have been trying to flee India 's major cities since the country went into a nationwide lockdown on March 24 . 
The lockdown has left many workers , who live far from home , without jobs or pay .
