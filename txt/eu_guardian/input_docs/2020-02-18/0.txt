Two Irish citizens have tested positive for coronavirus onboard the Diamond Princess cruise ship , Independent.ie has reported . 
More than 100 people have been tested in Ireland for the disease , but none have tested positive . 
There are six Irish nationals on the coronavirus hit cruise ship . 
The two who have tested positive share dual citizenship with another EU member state and are not normally resident in Ireland . 
They are being treated at a hospital in Japan . 
The Irish deputy prime minister , Simon Coveney , said : We are also in close contact with those who remain in quarantine on the ship . 
We are working with our EU partners and other like minded countries regarding options for their return .
