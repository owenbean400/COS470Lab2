As millions of Brazilians prepare to take to the streets for their country ’s annual carnival , Brazil ’s health minister has said his country currently has no specific plans for a coronavirus awareness campaign . 
But Luiz Henrique Mandetta has called on Brazilian revellers to exercise " respiratory etiquette " when the festivities kick off later this month . 
" What we are suggesting is respiratory etiquette , " he told reporters , according to the Valor Econômico newspaper , Brazil ’s answer to the FT . 
" Wash your hands several times a day ; if you sneeze , put your elbow out in front . " 
So far there have been no confirmed coronavirus cases in Brazil , but health authorities are preparing themselves for its arrival .
