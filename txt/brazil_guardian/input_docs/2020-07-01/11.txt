Colombia ’s confirmed number of infections has tipped across the 100,000 case threshold , as the country ’s quarantine measures roll on and intensive care units fill . 
They now number 102,009 , the health ministry said , 54,941 of which are active . 
Some 3,470 people have died . 
Wednesday also marked the highest ever daily increase in confirmed cases with an uptick of 4,163 .
