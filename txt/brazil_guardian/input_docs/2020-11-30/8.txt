Mexico is in a " bad shape " as coronavirus cases and deaths surge , according to the head of the World Health Organization . 
The country ’s death rally is now more than 105,500 and confirmed cases have passed 1.1 million . 
Public health experts believe it is likely to be significantly higher . 
The number of weekly deaths has risen from 2,000 in the week beginning 12 October , to 4,000 by last week . 
At least seven of Mexico City ’s 54 public hospitals are at capacity for Covid 19 beds and respirators . 
WHO director general Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus told a press conference in Geneva : " The number of increase in cases and deaths in Mexico is very worrisome . 
This shows Mexico is in [ a ] bad shape . "
