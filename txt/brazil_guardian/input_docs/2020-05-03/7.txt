There have been 4,588 new cases of the coronavirus in Brazil and 275 deaths over the last 24 hours , the health ministry said on Sunday , bringing total confirmed cases in the country to over 100,000 . 
The nation has now registered 101,147 confirmed cases of the virus and 7,025 deaths . 
The number of cases increased roughly 5 % on Sunday from the previous day , while deaths rose by roughly 4 % , the ministry said .
