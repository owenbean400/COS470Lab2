Good Morning Britain presenter Piers Morgan will not be hosting the programme tomorrow as he waits for the results of a Covid 19 test after experiencing a " mild symptom " . 
" On medical advice , and out of an abundance of caution for a mild symptom that arose in past 48hrs , I ’ve had a test for Covid 19 and so wo not be working on @ GMB until I get the result back , which should be tomorrow , " he tweeted .
