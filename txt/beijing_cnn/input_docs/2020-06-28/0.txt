China has reported 17 additional cases of coronavirus , according to the National Health Commission ( NHC ) . 
That number included 14 locally transmitted cases in Beijing , which is dealing with a fresh outbreak . 
The other three cases were imported cases . 
According to NHC , China has reported a total of 83,500 confirmed cases . 
A total of 78,451 patients have been discharged , and 4,634 people have died .
