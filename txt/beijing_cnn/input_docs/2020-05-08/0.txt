China 's National Health Commission on Friday reported only one new locally transmitted coronavirus cases and no additional deaths related to the virus . 
The commission reported 16 new asymptomatic cases . 
No deaths have been reported in China since April 15 . 
Here 's the breakdown of the latest numbers from Beijing : Total number of confirmed cases : 82,886 Patients who have recovered and been discharged : 77,993 Death toll : 4,633 Asymptomatic patients still under medical observation : 854
