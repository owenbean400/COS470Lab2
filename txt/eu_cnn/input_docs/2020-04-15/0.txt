The EU ’s top diplomat says he " deeply regrets " Donald Trump ’s decision to suspend funding for the World Health Organization ( WHO ) . 
The bloc ’s High Representative for Foreign Affairs , Josep Borrell , said : " There is no reason justifying this move at a moment when their efforts are needed more than ever to help contain & mitigate the coronavirus pandemic . 
Only by joining forces we can overcome this crisis that knows no borders . " 
Trump announced Tuesday he is halting funding to the WHO while a review is conducted . 
He said the review would cover the WHO 's " role in severely mismanaging and covering up the spread of coronavirus . "
