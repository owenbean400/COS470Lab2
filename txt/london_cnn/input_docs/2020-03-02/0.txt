A coronavirus cases has led to a secondary boys school in southwest London to be closed , after a member of staff caught the virus during a trip to Italy , Merton Council said in a statement . 
" We have been in discussion with Wimbledon College which has taken the decision to close as a number of staff are self isolating due to being close contacts of this confirmed case , " Dr . Yvonne Young , a consultant in communicable disease control at Public Health England ( PHE ) London , said in a statement . 
She added that there is " no information to suggest that there is an increased health risk to any pupils or their families at the college and no recommendation for them to self isolate . "
