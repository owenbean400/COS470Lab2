There are at least 10,962,835 cases of coronavirus in the US and at least 245,933 people have died from the virus , according to data from Johns Hopkins University . 
As of Sunday afternoon , 58,945 new cases and 335 new deaths have been reported in the US since midnight . 
The total includes cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia , and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases .
