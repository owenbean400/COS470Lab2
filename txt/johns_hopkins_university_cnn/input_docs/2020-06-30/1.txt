The United States reported at least 41,586 new coronavirus cases and 338 deaths on Monday , according to Johns Hopkins University . 
To date , the US has recorded at least 2,590,582 cases of Covid 19 , according to JHU 's tally of cases . 
At least 126,141 people have died from the virus nationwide . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases .
