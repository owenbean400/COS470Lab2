On Friday , the United States recorded 73,715 new cases of Covid 19 and 1,130 deaths , according to information provided by Johns Hopkins University . 
The US has now recorded a total of 4,112,531 coronavirus cases , of which at least 145,546 people have died . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases . 
The three worst hit states , New York , California and Florida , have each recorded more than 400,000 cases . 
Texas has recorded over 380,000 cases and the next worst hit state , New Jersey , has recorded more than 178,000 cases .
