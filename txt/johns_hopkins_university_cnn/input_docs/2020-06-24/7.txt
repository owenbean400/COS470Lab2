Yesterday marked the third highest number of new coronavirus cases reported in a single day in the US since the beginning of the pandemic This is based on the archive of numbers kept by Johns Hopkins University . 
At least 34,720 cases were reported yesterday . 
The second highest number — 34,756 cases — was reported on April 9 . 
The highest number of new cases was reported on April 24 , when at least 36,291 were recorded .
