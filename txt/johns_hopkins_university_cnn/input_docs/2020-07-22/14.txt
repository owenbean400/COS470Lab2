California added 12,807 coronavirus cases over the past day , Gov . 
Gavin Newsom announced at a news conference today . 
This comes after the state conducted more than 127,000 tests in one day . 
The positivity rate remains steady at 7.4 % over the past two weeks , but the one week rate is climbing and currently holds at 7.6 % . 
" Every decimal point causes some concern , " Newsom said .
