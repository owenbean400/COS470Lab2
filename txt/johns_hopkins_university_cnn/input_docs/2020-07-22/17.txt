Missouri Department of Health and Senior Services reported Wednesday a record single day increase in Covid 19 cases . 
The state reported 1,301 new Covid 19 cases , bringing the statewide total to 36,063 . 
" This month , we have been experiencing an increase in both testing and positive cases . 
The increase has caused a backlog of data processing and entry at the state level . 
Our team is working diligently to address our backlog with the support of newly trained team members , " the department tweeted Wednesday .
