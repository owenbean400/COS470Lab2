There are now more than 100,000 confirmed cases of the novel coronavirus , according to a count from Johns Hopkins University in the US . 
The university 's dashboard reported a total of 100,329 cases across the world this morning . 
Remember : This number is slightly different than the latest count from World Health Organization , which is reporting 98,202 cases around the world . 
Johns Hopkins and WHO report tallies at different times of the day , so the counts often differ .
