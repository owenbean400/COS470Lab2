Florida health officials are reporting at least 3,838 new coronavirus cases and approximately 219 new Covid 19 related deaths among Florida residents on Tuesday , according to data released by the Florida Department of Health ( FLDOH ) . 
Florida ’s highest number of deaths in a single day—276 deaths—was reported on August 11 . 
This is the tenth time Florida has reported more than 200 coronavirus related deaths in one day . 
The state is reporting at least 573,811 coronavirus cases among Florida residents and approximately 9,758 deaths , according to FLDOH data . 
Florida health officials report at least 579,932 total cases across the state . 
Note : These numbers were released by Florida ’s public health agency , and may not line up exactly in real time with CNN ’s database drawn from Johns Hopkins University and the Covid Tracking Project
