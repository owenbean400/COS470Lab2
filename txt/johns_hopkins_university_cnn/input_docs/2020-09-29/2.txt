The United States recorded 33,037 new coronavirus infections and 316 virus related deaths on Monday , according to Johns Hopkins University . 
The nationwide totals now stand at 7,148,045 cases , including 205,072 fatalities , per JHU 's tally . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases .
