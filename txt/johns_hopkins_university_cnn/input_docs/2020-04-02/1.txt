More than 5,000 people have died from the novel coronavirus in the United States , according to data from Johns Hopkins University . 
It is a grim milestone for America , which has seen a rapid rise in coronavirus cases and fatalities in recent weeks . 
The total number of deaths stands at 5,119 and at least 216,515 infections have been recorded , according to Johns Hopkins figures . 
Going forward , CNN will be reporting the Johns Hopkins University statistics on the coronavirus .
