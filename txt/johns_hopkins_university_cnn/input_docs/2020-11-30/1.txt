The number of Maryland coronavirus patients in the hospital is at a six month high , according to new figures released Monday by the state 's Department of Health . 
The state reports 1,527 Covid 19 patients in their hospitals , including 344 in intensive care . 
The last time Maryland saw a figure that high was May 14 . 
The seven day rolling average shows a positivity rate of 6.5 % , with 1,923 new cases reported in the last 24 hours . 
Maryland has confirmed 198,370 cases of Covid 19 , with 4,486 deaths .
