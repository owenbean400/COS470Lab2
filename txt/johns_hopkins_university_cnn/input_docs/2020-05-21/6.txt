At least 93,439 people have died in the US from coronavirus , according to Johns Hopkins University 's latest tally . 
There are at least 1,551,853 cases of the disease in the country . 
The US has the highest number of cases in the world . 
The totals includes cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases .
