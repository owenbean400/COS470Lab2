According to Johns Hopkins University 's tally of cases in the United States , there have been at least 6,039,169 cases reported since the start of the pandemic . 
At least 183,733 people have died in the US from coronavirus . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases . 
So far on Monday , Johns Hopkins has reported 8,582 new cases and 136 reported deaths in the US .
