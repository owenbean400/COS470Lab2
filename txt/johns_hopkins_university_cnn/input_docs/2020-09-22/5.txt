The United States has surpassed 200,000 deaths from coronavirus , according to Johns Hopkins University . 
The first death from coronavirus in the US was reported on Feb . 29 . 
Fifty four days later , on April 23 , the US reported 50,000 deaths from Covid 19 . 
Just 29 days later , on May 23 , the nation crossed 100,000 deaths . 
It took 65 days to reach 150,000 deaths on July 28 . 
It has taken 55 days to reach 200,000 deaths .
