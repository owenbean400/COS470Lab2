The United States reported 170,161 new coronavirus cases and 1,848 virus related deaths on Wednesday , according to Johns Hopkins University . 
At least 11,527,483 Covid 19 cases and 250,520 deaths have now been reported in the US , according to the university 's tally . 
The totals include cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as repatriated cases .
