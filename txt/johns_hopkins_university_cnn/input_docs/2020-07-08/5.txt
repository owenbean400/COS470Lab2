The US has recorded more than 3 million coronavirus cases since the pandemic began , according to Johns Hopkins University numbers . 
That 's almost two times more than the second hardest hit nation , Brazil , which has reported more than 1.6 million cases . 
Here 's a quick timeline of how quickly the virus spread in the US : Jan . 21 : The US reports its first reported case . 
99 days later : At least 1 million Americans have been infected 43 days later : The US reports more than 2 million cases 28 days later : The US reached 3 million cases
