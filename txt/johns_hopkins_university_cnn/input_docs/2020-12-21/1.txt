Connecticut 's hospitalizations and positivity rate are moving downward , according to Gov . 
Ned Lamont at a news conference on Monday . 
Hospitalizations are continuing to trend downward and are currently at 1,143 , which is 24 less than what they were on Friday . 
The positivity rate is 5.18 % and was at 6.85 % on Friday . 
Lamont also reported 4,595 new Covid 19 cases and 95 new deaths . 
A total of 7,761 doses of the Covid 19 vaccine have been administered in the state so far , Lamont said . 
Moderna vaccine deliveries will begin in the state starting today , he said .
