As the United States continues to battle the coronavirus pandemic , a majority of states are showing an increase in new cases compared to the previous week . 
According to Johns Hopkins University data : 27 states are showing upward trends 14 states are showing steady trends 9 states are showing downward trends The US is currently averaging 42,785 new Covid 19 cases a day , which is down 1 % from last week . 
The country continues to lead the world in total cases , with more than 7 million coronavirus cases .
