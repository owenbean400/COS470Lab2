Minnesota should increase testing for Covid 19 before it considers reopening and allowing everyone to return to work , Gov . 
Tim Walz said at a news conference on Wednesday . 
Walz lamented the lack of testing on a national scale , but said he thought Minnesota had an opportunity for more antibody testing . 
There should be no rush to send everyone back to work , he said . 
Minnesota has reported at least 1,809 confirmed coronavirus cases , including 87 deaths , according to Johns Hopkins University .
