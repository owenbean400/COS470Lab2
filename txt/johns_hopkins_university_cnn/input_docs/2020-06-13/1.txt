There are at least 2,048,986 cases of coronavirus in the United States and at least 114,669 people have died in the country from coronavirus , according to Johns Hopkins University 's tally of cases in the United States . 
The totals includes cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other U.S . territories , as well as repatriated cases .
