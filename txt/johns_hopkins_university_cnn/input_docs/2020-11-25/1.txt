The United States reported 2,146 deaths from Covid 19 on Tuesday , according to data from Johns Hopkins University . 
This is the 21st time that the US has added more than 2,000 coronavirus fatalities in a day and the highest new death numbers since May . 
The most deaths in a single day were recorded April 15 -- 2,603 people . 
The US also reported 172,935 new coronavirus infections Tuesday , according to JHU . 
The nationwide totals now stand at 12,591,163 confirmed cases and 259,925 deaths .
