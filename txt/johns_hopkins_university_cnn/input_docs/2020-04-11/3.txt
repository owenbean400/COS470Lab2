The US now has at least 501,301 cases of the novel coronavirus and at least 18,758 related deaths , according to data from Johns Hopkins University . 
On Friday , 35,551 new cases were reported and 2,074 people died . 
That 's the biggest single day jump in US deaths so far . 
The total number of US cases come from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as all cases repatriated from abroad . 
Wyoming is the only state yet to report a coronavirus death .
