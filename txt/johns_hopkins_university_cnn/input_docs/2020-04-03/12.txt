At least 245,213 cases of Covid 19 have been recorded in the United States , according to Johns Hopkins University 's tally . 
Of those , 5,983 patients have died . 
On Thursday , 28,491 new cases and 846 deaths were recorded , according to Johns Hopkins . 
The total includes cases from all 50 states , the District of Columbia and other US territories , as well as all repatriated cases . 
Wyoming is the only state not to have reported a death from coronavirus .
