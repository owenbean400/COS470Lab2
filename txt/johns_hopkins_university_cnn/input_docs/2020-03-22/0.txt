More than 300,000 people are infected with coronavirus around the world , according to a tally by Johns Hopkins University ( JHU ) . 
That 's a huge jump in the numbers . 
One week ago , JHU was reporting 156,396 confirmed cases . 
Meanwhile , the number of deaths from Covid 19 has more than doubled from one week ago , when there were 5,833 deaths . 
Today , there are nearly 13,000 , according to JHU .
