Travelers arriving in Vietnam will be quarantined for 14 days to help stop the spread of the novel coronavirus , according to state run newspaper Chinhphu . 
The measure applies to Vietnamese nationals and foreigners -- except those traveling for diplomatic and " official " purposes . 
People are required to self quarantine at places of residence under the supervision of local authorities . 
An army spokesperson on Wednesday said the country had prepared 140 quarantine areas to accommodate up to 44,718 people , according to Chinhphu . 
Vietnam has 91 confirmed coronavirus cases , according to the latest data from Johns Hopkins University . 
No fatalities have been reported in the country .
