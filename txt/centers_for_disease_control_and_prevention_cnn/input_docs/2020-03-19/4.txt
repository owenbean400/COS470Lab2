The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention has loosened its guidelines to allow health care workers who have been exposed to COVID 19 to continue working as long as they wear a mask , according to CNN 's Dr . Sanjay Gupta . 
Within the guidelines , the CDC says bandanas and scarves could be used in place of a mask , Gupta said . 
The reason for these guidelines is due to a lack of personal protective equipment and not enough health care workers , Gupta said .
