Children can carry coronavirus in their noses and throats for weeks even if they do not show any symptoms , which might explain how the virus can spread silently , researchers in South Korea reported Friday . 
" Interestingly , this study aligns with adult data in which up to 40 % of adults may remain asymptomatic in the face of infection , " Dr . Roberta De Biasi and Dr . Meghan Delaney , both of Children 's National Hospital in Washington , DC , wrote in an accompanying editorial . 
Neither was involved in the research .
