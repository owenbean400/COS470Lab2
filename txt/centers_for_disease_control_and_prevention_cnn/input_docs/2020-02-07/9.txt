Royal Caribbean said in a statement that " none of the four guests being tested by CDC " who were aboard the ship that docked in New Jersey " showed any clinical signs or symptoms of coronavirus . " 
The companies statement said one passenger " had tested positive onboard for Influenza A . " 
The company said their records indicate " the guests had not been in China since January 26 - 14 days ago . " Royal Caribbean says it will now have stricter protocols which will include " mandatory specialized health screenings performed " on guests who report " feeling unwell " or with " flu like symptoms , " anyone who traveled through mainland " China or Hong Kong in the past 15 days , " or had contact with someone who did . 
Test results from the CDC are expected as early as Saturday , the company said .
