Maryland Gov . 
Larry Hogan announced that the state will be allowed to reopen further on Friday . 
Phase two of his Roadmap to Recovery will permit indoor dining to resume at 50 % capacity , while maintaining " distancing and following strict public health requirements consistent with the CDC , the FDA and the National Restaurant Association , " Hogan said at a news briefing on Wednesday . 
Phase two allows outdoor graduation ceremonies to be held with the proper capacity and social distancing measures . 
Outdoor amusement parks and rides and miniature golf and go carts will also reopen safety protocols .
