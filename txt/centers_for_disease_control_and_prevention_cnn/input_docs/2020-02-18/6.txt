An American who was evacuated on a US chartered jet from a cruise ship docked in Japan told CNN that she was not aware that other passengers on the plane had tested positive for novel coronavirus until they landed . 
Sarah Arana was one of more than 300 US citizens evacuated from the Diamond Princess and flown to the United States . 
Fourteen passengers from the Diamond Princess had tested positive for coronavirus before they boarded the flight . 
But Arana felt she was in good hands while on the evacuation flight and when she landed on US soil . 
" On the plane we were with specialists ... very knowledgeable doctors and CDC professionals , " she said . 
" I have no doubt that they did everything with extreme caution . "
