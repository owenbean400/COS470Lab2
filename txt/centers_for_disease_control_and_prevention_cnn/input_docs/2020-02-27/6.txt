As the number of coronavirus cases increase , reports are showing that there is a growing worldwide mask shortage . 
This has people asking themselves if they should be wearing one . 
Tariro Mzezewa , a New York Times travel reporter , tells CNN the measure is not necessary unless you are sick or are interacting with sick people . 
The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention does not recommend Americans wear surgical masks in public . 
Surgical masks are effective against respiratory infections but not airborne infections .
