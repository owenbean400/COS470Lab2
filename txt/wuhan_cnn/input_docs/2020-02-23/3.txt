Chinese President Xi Jinping personally wrote to Bill Gates after the billionaire 's foundation committed $ 100 million in emergency funding to help China bolster epidemiological research , emergency intervention , and the research and development of drugs , vaccines , and diagnostics , according to state media . 
Xi has faced criticism over his handling of the coronavirus crisis , after it was revealed last week that he knew about the virus spreading in the central Chinese city of Wuhan earlier than first thought . 
Officials in Wuhan had previously apologized for their poor handling of the early stages of the outbreak , which allowed infections to spread nationwide .
