In the coming days , hundreds of millions of Chinese are expected to begin traveling across the country and overseas as the annual Lunar New Year break gets fully underway . 
The holiday is most important in the Chinese calendar and will see people from across the country cram themselves into homebound trains , buses and planes for family reunions More than 60 routes connect Wuhan with other countries , including direct flights to New York , San Francisco , Sydney , Paris and London , as well as over a hundred internal flights to other major Chinese cities .
