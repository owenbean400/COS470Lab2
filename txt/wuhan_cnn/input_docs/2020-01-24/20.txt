The Hubei government has released further details regarding seven cases among the 24 deaths related to the Wuhan coronavirus in the province . 
Among them is a 36 year old man , surnamed Li , who is the youngest person so far to have died as a result of the virus . 
Li was hospitalized on January 9 after three days of fever , and died on January 23 . 
There is no mention of him having any pre existing conditions . 
Previously , the youngest confirmed fatality was 48 years old , with most casualties in their 60s or older .
