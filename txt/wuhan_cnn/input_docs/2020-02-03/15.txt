US President Donald Trump 's national security adviser , Robert O 'Brien , on Sunday said that at this time " there 's no reason for Americans to panic " about the Wuhan coronavirus . 
O 'Brien also said the virus is a " top priority " for Trump and the administration is " taking steps to keep Americans safe . " 
The White House on Thursday announced a coronavirus task force that " will lead the Administration 's efforts to monitor , contain , and mitigate the spread of the virus . " 
It is lead by Health and Human Services Secretary Alex Azar and includes members from the National Institutes of Health , the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention , Department of Homeland Security and others .
