The Wuhan coronavirus has killed more people inside mainland China than the coronavirus responsible for the SARS outbreak in 2003 . 
China 's National Health Commission ( NHC ) reported the Wuhan outbreak was responsible for 361 deaths by the end of day Sunday . 
SARS killed 349 people in mainland China , according to the NHC . 
SARS was responsible for the deaths of 774 people worldwide . 
To date , only one person has been killed outside mainland China from the Wuhan coronavirus .
