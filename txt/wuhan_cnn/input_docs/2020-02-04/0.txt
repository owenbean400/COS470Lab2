There is not a single face exposed in Macao 's cavernous Galaxy casino . 
Everyone is wearing a mask , including the croupiers , waitresses and security guards -- who happen to vastly outnumber the scattered customers gambling at blackjack and roulette tables . 
Visitors only momentarily drop their masks at the entrances to the casino , to pose for thermal cameras on the lookout for the deadly Wuhan coronavirus that has killed hundreds of people in mainland China and infected thousands more . 
The outbreak has left the free wheeling , semi autonomous Chinese territory of Macao shell shocked . 
Last year the city received almost 40 million visitors . 
Now , streets and squares once teeming with tourists from mainland China are empty . 
Ambulances roam the city , operated by emergency workers dressed in hazardous materials suits .
