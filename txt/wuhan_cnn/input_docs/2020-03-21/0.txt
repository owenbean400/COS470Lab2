Different times call for different measures . 
When Covid 19 hit China , I was concerned , as were many public health professionals , about what could happen and urged rapid action to understand more and prepare . 
But few of us anticipated the catastrophic impact the new virus has had in Wuhan , in Italy and may soon have in many other places . 
For most people , there is simply no frame of reference for this pandemic . 
Never in our lifetime has there been an infectious disease threat as devastating to society . 
Never in our lifetime have we seen a rich country like Italy face the need to ration respirators . 
And never have we seen the fear that millions of health care workers around the world feel about being infected by the virus -- justified fear we must address .
