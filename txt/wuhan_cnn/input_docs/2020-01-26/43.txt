The Los Angeles County Department of Public Health has confirmed the first case of coronavirus in the county , according to a statement from the department . 
It is the fourth confirmed case in the United States . 
Public Health officials in the county said there is no immediate threat to the public and no special precautions are required , but urged residents in Los Angeles to " practice good public health hygiene . "
