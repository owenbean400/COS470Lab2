A 2 year old girl has been diagnosed with coronavirus in Singapore , the city state 's Ministry of Health said . 
The toddler had been evacuated from Wuhan on January 30 and placed under quarantine until the infection was confirmed on Monday morning . 
A 37 year old Singaporean with " no recent travel history to China " was also confirmed to have the virus on Tuesday . 
That brings the total number of coronavirus cases in Singapore up to 45 , according to the ministry .
