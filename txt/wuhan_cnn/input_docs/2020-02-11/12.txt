A three month old baby girl and 55 year old woman are the latest people in Vietnam to contract coronavirus , according the country 's state run Vietnam News Agency ( VNA ) . 
The two patients are among 10 people in the northern Vinh Phuc province to have been infected . 
Across Vietnam , 15 people have the virus . 
The baby was tested after her grandmother was diagnosed with the virus . 
She had previously spent four days at her grandmother 's house . 
The baby is in quarantine and in a stable condition . 
The 55 year old woman is the neighbor of an infected patient who had recently been to Wuhan . 
She was in a stable condition at a local clinic on Sunday
