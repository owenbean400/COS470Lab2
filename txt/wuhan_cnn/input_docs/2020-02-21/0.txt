Two months after the novel coronavirus outbreak began , there are now at least 75,465 cases across mainland China . 
The country 's death toll stands at 2,236 . 
By far the vast majority of cases are in Hubei province -- its capital Wuhan is the epicenter of the outbreak . 
According to Hubei 's health authority , 62,422 cases of the coronavirus have been recorded in the province alone , with 2,144 deaths . 
There are just over 13,000 cases of the virus in mainland China outside Hubei and 92 deaths . 
Here are the other worst hit provinces as of February 20 : Guangdong : 1,332 cases , 5 deaths Henan : 1,265 cases , 19 deaths Zhejiang : 1,175 cases , 0 deaths Hunan : 1,010 cases , 4 deaths Anhui : 987 cases , 6 deaths
