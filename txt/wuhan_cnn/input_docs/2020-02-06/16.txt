More than 7,300 people are being quarantined on two cruise ships off Hong Kong and Japan because of concerns that passengers and crew have been exposed to the Wuhan coronavirus . 
One of the vessels -- the Diamond Princess -- is anchored off the coast of Yokohama , near Tokyo . 
It has 1,045 crew and 2,666 passengers -- including 428 Americans -- on board , and ended its planned 14 day itinerary early . 
Japan 's Health Ministry said that 20 people on board the cruise liner had been infected . 
Passengers on board will remain under quarantine for at least 14 days , " as required by the Ministry of Health , " the cruise company said in a statement .
