The Canadian government is working to help nearly 200 Canadians who want leave China and return home , the Public Health Agency of Canada said . 
It is unclear when they will be able to fly out of China . 
Health officials did not discuss what kind of quarantine will be imposed once the group arrives in Canada . 
Three cases of coronavirus have been confirmed in Canada . 
Two of the patients recently traveled to Wuhan , the agency had said . 
It said the risk of the new coronavirus spreading within Canada remained low .
