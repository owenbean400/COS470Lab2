Iceland has received its first shipment of the Pfizer Bio NTech coronavirus vaccine , a spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs confirmed to CNN on Monday . 
According to the spokesperson , an aircraft carrying Iceland ’s first shipment of the vaccine landed at Keflavík International Airport Monday morning . 
It arrived from Amsterdam in the Netherlands . 
The shipment included 10,000 doses of the vaccine , which are expected to be rolled out from Tuesday . 
Earlier on Monday , Pfizer told CNN that it had had to reschedule a limited number of deliveries of the vaccine due to " minor logistical issues , " with some countries in Europe now expected to receive their deliveries on Tuesday , rather than Monday .
