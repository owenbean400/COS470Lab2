The United States could be back to normal by next summer or early fall if everyone gets a Covid 19 vaccine , Dr . Anthony Fauci , the director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases , predicted Thursday . 
The US Food and Drug Administration ’s Vaccines and Related Biological Products Advisory Committee voted to recommend emergency use authorization ( EUA ) for Pfizer ’s Covid 19 vaccine Thursday . 
The FDA is expected to approve an EUA for the vaccine in the next couple of days .
