Kentucky reports 3,558 new Covid 19 cases , according to a release from Gov . 
Andy Beshear . 
The test positivity rate across the state is 8.79 % . 
The state reports a total of 2,192 coronavirus related deaths since the start of the pandemic , with 1,711 people currently hospitalized with the virus . 
" Late yesterday , the FDA approved Pfizer ’s vaccine for COVID 19 . 
That ’s great news , but it will still be some time before everyone can get vaccinated and we have to stay vigilant until that time , " Beshear said .
