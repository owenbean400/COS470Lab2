We reported earlier on efforts by the Trump administration to discredit the White House ’s infectious diseases expert Dr Anthony Fauci . 
Donald Trump has now claimed to have a " very good relationship with Dr Fauci " , while his press secretary Kayleigh Mc Enany denied that officials had shared opposition research with reporters .
