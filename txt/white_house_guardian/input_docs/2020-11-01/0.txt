Scott Atlas , a top coronavirus adviser to the White House , has apologised for giving an interview to Russia ’s Kremlin backed television station RT , saying he was unaware the outlet was a registered foreign agent in the United States . 
Atlas , a neuroradiologist and member of the White House coronavirus task force , appeared on the channel on Saturday and criticised coronavirus lockdowns measures , calling them an " epic failure " at stopping the virus ’ spread."I recently did an interview with RT and was unaware they are a registered foreign agent , " Atlas tweeted . 
" I regret doing the interview and apologize for allowing myself to be taken advantage of."I especially apologize to the national security community who is working hard to defend us , " Atlas said . RT registered as a foreign agent three years ago .
