The Tokyo 2020 Olympic games look increasingly like they will become Tokyo 2021 . 
But – perhaps in an effort to provide those under lockdown with consolation prize edge of their sofa entertainment – there is still no official announcement from Japan or the International Olympic Committee . 
What we are hearing is that Japan ’s government is negotiating with the IOC to postpone the games by a maximum one year – as reported in the Sankei newspaper on Tuesday .
