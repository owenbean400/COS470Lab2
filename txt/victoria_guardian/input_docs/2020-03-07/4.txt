Regarding the latest case in Victoria , Australia , a doctor who treated 70 patients after showing flu like symptoms – he is now a confirmed case of Covid 19 , Dr Sutton , the state ’s chief health officer is asked : How worried are you that given that the doctor was treating this patient in close proximity , that we will have more cases of coronavirus ? 
He responds : We need to be alert to the possibility , that is why would be monitoring them on a daily basis . 
All of them have been contacted en masse this morning , they will be called again through the course of today . 
They will be isolated and with respect to the ongoing risk from them in developing illness , we can manage that , but yes , we have to be alert to the possibility of developing illness .
