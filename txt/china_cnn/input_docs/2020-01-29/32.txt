Italy will begin repatriating its citizens from Wuhan this week , Foreign Minister Luigi Di Maio announced Wednesday . 
" The Italian government is committed to giving maximum support to our compatriots in China . 
Tomorrow a flight will leave for the repatriation of the Italians who are in Wuhan , " Di Maio tweeted . 
" The aircraft , scheduled to depart from Italy tomorrow once the necessary authorizations have been acquired by the Chinese , will reach Wuhan airport with specialized medical personnel , nurses and adequate medical equipment on board to ensure safe transport . 
Upon arrival in Italy , compatriots will follow a health protocol defined by the Ministry of Health , " Italy 's foreign ministry said in a statement .
