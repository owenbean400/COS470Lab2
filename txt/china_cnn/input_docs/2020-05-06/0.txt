China recorded two new coronavirus cases on Tuesday , both imported from overseas , according to the country 's National Health Commission . 
In addition , 20 new asymptomatic cases were recorded . 
A total of 903 asymptomatic patients are still under medical observation . 
The total number of cases officially recorded in mainland China now stands at 82,883 , including 4,633 deaths . 
The vast majority of patients have recovered and been discharged from hospital , the NHC said . 
Some 339 cases remain active .
