More cases : The novel coronavirus has now infected more than 67,000 people globally , the vast majority in mainland China . 
The death toll is over 1,520 , including four people outside mainland China . 
First death in Europe : A Chinese tourist who tested positive for the virus died in France , health officials confirmed on Saturday . 
Medical workers at risk : China says over 1,700 medical workers have been infected by the virus , and six have died . 
Spike in numbers : There 's been a spike in numbers because China changed how cases are tabulated . 
The total will now include people who demonstrate all the symptoms of Covid 19 but have not been tested , or are believed to have falsely tested negative .
