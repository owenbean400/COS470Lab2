The German Foreign Office has put out an updated travel advisory for China regarding the Wuhan coronavirus . 
It is advising German citizens to : consider postponing travel to China if it 's not urgent register at one of the German government crisis preventive lists if you 're in China avoid travel to the province of Hubei plan for travel restrictions in the area obey the orders of local security forces take into consideration the World Health Organization advisories as well as those from the Robert Koch institute , the German government agency responsible for disease control and prevention .
