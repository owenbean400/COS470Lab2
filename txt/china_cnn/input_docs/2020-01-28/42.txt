Three patients in Washington , DC were tested for novel coronavirus , of which one tested negative and two are pending results from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention , according to DC Health . 
DC Health said there are currently no cases of the virus in DC and the current risk to DC residents is low . 
About the virus : The novel coronavirus , which has sickened thousands and killed more than 100 people in China , belongs to a large family of viruses that mostly sicken animals . 
But this coronavirus , like SARS and MERS , " jumped the species barrier " to infect people on a large scale , the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said .
