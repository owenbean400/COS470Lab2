A second person has died of the coronavirus in the semiautonomous Chinese financial hub of Hong Kong , the local hospital authority announced this morning . 
The victim was a 70 year old old man who was diagnosed with the virus on February 14 before being admitted to Princess Margaret Hospital . 
There are now 62 confirmed cases of the virus across Hong Kong and two deaths . 
The first Hong Kong victim of the coronavirus was a 39 year old man with an underlying illness who died on February 4 and had an underlying illness . 
He took the high speed train from Hong Kong to the virus epicenter of Wuhan on January 21 and returned to the city on January 23 . 
Hong Kong has temporarily closed some of its borders with China and stopped issuing travel permits to mainland tourists .
