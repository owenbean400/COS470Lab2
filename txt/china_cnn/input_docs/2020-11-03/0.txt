Hong Kong will require travelers from any country , except mainland China , to quarantine in a hotel for 14 days upon arrival there . 
The new measures will go into effect on November 13 , " and remain until further notice , " the government said in a statement on Tuesday . 
Travelers in transit through Hong Kong will be exempt from these measures . 
Hotel quarantine previously only applied to travelers from specified high risk countries . 
On Tuesday , Hong Kong 's Department of Health reported nine new confirmed Covid 19 cases , bringing its total to 5,346 . 
" Among the newly reported cases announced , six had a travel history during the incubation period , " the government statement added .
