The first ever Jakarta E Prix has been postponed due to the global spread of the novel coronavirus . 
The event is the third Formula E race of the season to be called off . 
Formula E has already canceled E Prix races in Sanya , China , scheduled for March 21 and another in Rome that was set for April 4 . 
The Jakarta race was to be held on June 6 . 
On Wednesday , Indonesia confirmed its first death from the virus and said its number of cases had risen to 27 from 19 .
