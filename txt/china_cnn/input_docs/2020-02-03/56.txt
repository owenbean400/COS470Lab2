A husband and wife , both aged 57 , have been diagnosed with Wuhan coronavirus in California 's San Benito County , according to a statement from local officials . 
This raises the number of confirmed cases in California to six , and 11 in the United States . 
The husband recently traveled from the central Chinese city of Wuhan , epicenter of the outbreak . 
However the wife did not -- a case of person to person transmission . 
The San Benito County Public Health Services has provided guidance to the couple for home isolation and is closely monitoring their medical condition , according to the statement . 
Neither patient is in hospital .
