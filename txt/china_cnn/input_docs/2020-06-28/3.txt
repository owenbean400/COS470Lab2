Chinese industrial firms have posted profit gains for the first time in 2020 , the National Bureau of Statistics said Sunday . 
In May , firms reported a 6 % rise in profits , year on year , the National Bureau of Statistics said Sunday in a statement . 
By comparison , profits in April fell by 4.3 % , compared to the same month last year . 
The bureau said May 's results are an indication that business activity is slowly improving . 
Lockdowns across China in February to contain the coronavirus outbreak caused many businesses to stop working .
