Israel 's Ministry of Health urged citizens to reconsider non essential travel abroad due to heightened fears over the spread of coronavirus in Europe and across the globe . 
The Ministry of Health also advised against travel to conferences and events with international congregations , including religious events , the ministry said . 
Additionally , the ministry advised against holding international conferences in Israel . 
Israel also added Italy to a restricted travel list , comprised of China , Japan , Singapore , South Korea , Macau , Thailand and Hong Kong . 
Any foreign national who has been to any of these countries within the last 14 days will not be granted entry into Israel . 
Any Israeli citizens returning from these countries must remain self quarantined for at least 14 days .
