Asian Football Conference ( AFC ) Champions League soccer games involving four Chinese clubs have been postponed due to the outbreak , the AFC announced Tuesday after an emergency meeting in Kuala Lumpur , Malaysia . 
The first three match days -- involving Beijing FC , Guangzhou Evergrande , Shanghai Shenhua and Shanghai SIPG -- will be rescheduled to April and May . " We came together in difficult circumstances to find solutions to allow us to play football while ensuring we protect the safety and security of all players , officials , stakeholders and fans , " Dato ' Windsor John , AFC General Secretary , said in statement . 
" We also accept that this coronavirus is a much bigger issue than just football and we wish all those who have been affected a speedy return to health . "
