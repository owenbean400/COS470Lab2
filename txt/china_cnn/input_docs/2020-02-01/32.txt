Delta has decided to temporarily suspend all US to China flying beginning Feb . 2 due to ongoing concerns related to the coronavirus , the airline said in a news release today . 
The last China bound flight departing the US will leave today with the last return flight back to the US departing China on Feb . 2 , the company said in the release . 
The airline had previously announced a plan to suspend operations effective Feb . 6 but advanced that timeline based on new US requirements that will deny entry to foreign nationals who have traveled to China within the past two weeks , or subject to possible quarantine those US citizens who have been in China â€™s Hubei province , the company said .
