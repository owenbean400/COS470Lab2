World Health Organization Director General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus announced in a series of Tweets on Sunday that he is headed to China to assist with response efforts to the coronavirus . 
" We are working 24/7 to support [ China ] & its people during this difficult time & remain in close contact with affected countries , with our regional & country offices deeply involved . 
@ WHO is updating all countries on the situation & providing specific guidance on what to do to respond , " Ghebreyesus said in a separate tweet . 
" Building on experience and systems already in place for related pathogens , @ WHO has activated global networks of experts , quickly developed advice for countries everywhere , and is working with them to activate their response systems . 
# coronavirus "
