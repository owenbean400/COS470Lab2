The Dow and the broader stock market ended deep in the red on Friday as fears over the spread of the Wuhan coronavirus tainted investor sentiment . 
Stocks started the day higher as it appeared markets were shrugging off the coronavirus news , but took a turn for the worse after a second case was confirmed on US soil in Illinois . 
The first case was confirmed on Tuesday in Washington state . 
Both patients had traveled to the central Chinese city of Wuhan , where the virus outbreak originated .
