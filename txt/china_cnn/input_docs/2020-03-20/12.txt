New York Gov . 
Andrew Cuomo said today that the state now has 7,102 positive cases of coronavirus . 
New York has tested more than 32,000 people , Cuomo said . 
Cuomo noted that since the state is now doing more tests you 're " going to get more positive cases . " 
He said the state has " the testing so high that we 're testing per capita more than China or South Korea . " 
New York has done a " great job ramping up " testing , Cuomo said . 
He pointed out that 10,000 people were tested last night .
