The coronavirus pandemic has pushed the world 's economies into historic slumps . 
But China is bucking the trend . 
The world 's second largest economy has been in recovery mode for months . 
Now , consumers are starting to spend more , pushing retail sales up to 3.36 trillion yuan ( $ 495 billion ) in August , a 0.5 % increase over the previous year . 
While small , the gain marks the first time sales have increased in 2020 . 
Chinese authorities touted the uptick at a monthly press conference Tuesday , and pointed out that the country is seeing economic improvement elsewhere , too .
