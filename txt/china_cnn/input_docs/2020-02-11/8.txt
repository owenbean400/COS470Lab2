Two Japanese citizens who had initially tested negative for coronavirus have now been diagnosed with the disease . 
Japan now has confirmed 163 cases . 
The two new patients , both men , were evacuated from China to Japan . 
One is in his 50s and the other in his 40s , the ministry said , and both have no connection to the Diamond Princess , the cruise ship docked off the coast of Yokohama on which 135 have been infected by the virus . 
The men were retested after developing fevers .
