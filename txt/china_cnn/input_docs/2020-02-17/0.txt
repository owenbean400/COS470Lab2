More cases : The novel coronavirus has infected more than 71,000 people around the world , mostly in mainland China . 
The death toll is 1,873 , including five people outside mainland China . 
Cruise ship evacuation : Another 99 cases have been confirmed aboard the quarantined Diamond Princess cruise ship in Japan . 
The US has evacuated more than 300 Americans from the ship . 
Canada , Italy and Hong Kong are also sending flights for their citizens .
