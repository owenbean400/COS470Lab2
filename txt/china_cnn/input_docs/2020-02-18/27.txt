Hong Kong has reported one more confirmed case of the novel coronavirus on Tuesday , bringing the citywide total to 62 cases , according to the Hong Kong Health Department . 
The new case is a 58 year old man who developed fever and chills on February 11 . 
According to the Health Department , he traveled to Zhongshan , China , and Macao during the incubation period . 
He is currently in stable condition .
