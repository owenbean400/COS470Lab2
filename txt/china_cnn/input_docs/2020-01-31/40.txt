Sweden has confirmed its first case of coronavirus , a woman in Jönköping county who had visited the Wuhan area of China , Sweden ’s Public Health Authority said Friday . 
When the woman landed in Sweden on January 24 , she was free of symptoms of the infection , but later developed a cough and contacted a local hospital . 
She was isolated in the hospital 's infection clinic , the agency said in a statement , but is not seriously ill .
