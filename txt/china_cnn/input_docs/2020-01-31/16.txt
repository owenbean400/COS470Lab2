Kenya Airways has become the latest airline to suspend flights to and from China . 
" The safety of our customers and staff remains our highest priority , " it added . 
Other airlines have also suspended flights : British Airways , United Airlines , Air Canada , American Airlines , Air Asia , Cathay Pacific , Air India , Indi Go , Lufthansa and Finnair have announced plans to slash the number of flights they are operating to China or stop flying to the country entirely . 
Other airlines like Delta are offering customers refunds or change fee waivers .
