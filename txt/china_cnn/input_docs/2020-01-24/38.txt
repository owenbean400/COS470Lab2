There are now three confirmed cases of the virus in Singapore , according to its Ministry of Health ( MOH ) . 
The second confirmed patient , who has been placed in an isolation ward , is a 53 year old female Chinese national from Wuhan who arrived in Singapore with her family on January 21 , the ministry wrote in a statement . 
Her condition remains stable . 
The third confirmed patient is a 37 year old male Chinese national from Wuhan , the statement said . 
He is the son of the first confirmed case , a 66 year old Chinese national who is also from Wuhan , the MOH wrote . 
The ministry added that out of 44 suspected cases in Singapore , 13 have tested negative , three tested positive . 
The results of 28 cases are pending , it wrote .
