China has reported another 35 deaths and 573 cases today -- the vast majority from Hubei province , the epicenter of the coronavirus outbreak . 
Here 's the breakdown : Deaths : In mainland China : 2,870 Outside China : 106 Global total : 2,976 Cases : In mainland China : 79,824 Outside China : 6,676 Global total : 86,500 The deaths outside mainland China have so far been in Iran , Italy , South Korea , Japan , Hong Kong , France , the Philippines , Taiwan and the United States . 
China 's National Health Commission said 41,625 patients in the country have recovered and been discharged from hospital .
