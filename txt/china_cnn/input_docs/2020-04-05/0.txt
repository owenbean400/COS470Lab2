Thirty patients were reported to have contracted the novel coronavirus in mainland China on Saturday , according to country 's National Health Commission . 
Twenty five of those cases were imported from abroad . 
This raises the national case total to 81,669 . 
Three deaths were reported on Friday , bringing the national toll to 3,329 . 
Nearly 77,000 patients have recovered and been discharged from hospital . 
Additionally , 47 new asymptomatic infections were reported , including 16 imported cases . 
There are currently 1,024 asymptomatic cases under medical observation , among which 244 are imported .
