More cases : China has recorded 5,090 more cases of the novel coronavirus , bringing the global total to 64,435 . 
The death toll from the coronavirus is at least 1,383 , including three people outside mainland China . 
About the spiking numbers : China 's government said the spike in cases was due to a change in how cases are tabulated . 
The total will now include people who demonstrate all the symptoms of Covid 19 but have not been tested or are believed to have falsely tested negative . 
Medical workers at risk : China says over 1,700 medical workers have been infected by the virus , and six have died .
