China 's National Health Commission ( NHC ) has reported two new cases of the coronavirus and no deaths for the fifth consecutive day . 
One of the new cases was imported from abroad and the other was locally transmitted in northern Shanxi province . 
Twelve asymptomatic infections were also reported . 
China previously did not include those patients not showing symptoms in some of its tallies . 
Here 's the country breakdown , according to the NHC : Total confirmed infections : 82,877 Total deaths : 4,633 Total recovered and discharged : 77,713 Active cases remaining : 531
