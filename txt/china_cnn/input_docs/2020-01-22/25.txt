The Lunar New Year -- the most important festival in the Chinese calendar -- is just three days away , but in the Chinese city of Wuhan , there are few reasons to celebrate . 
A deadly outbreak of a new coronavirus emerged in this city of 11 million people last month . 
Within weeks , the virus has killed nine people , sickened hundreds and spread as far as the United States .
