US Health and Human Services Secretary Alex Azar responded to questions about mixed messaging coming from the White House about the coronavirus pandemic in an interview with News Nation 's Marni Hughes posted online on Wednesday . 
Azar was asked whether there was anything to " add clarity and accuracy " on . 
" That is the message of this administration and the strategy of this administration for dealing with the coronavirus and that is from the President through me , " Azar added . 
According to News Nation , the interview – which also included US Surgeon General Dr . Jerome Adams and White House coronavirus task force member Dr . Deborah Birx – is the first time these top health three health leaders fighting the coronavirus pandemic have sat down for an interview together .
