Dr . Deborah Birx , the White House coronavirus response coordinator , said Thursday that it is possible for the country to get coronavirus case counts low enough for children to go back to school . 
Speaking during an interview on " Fox & Friends , " Birx said there are school districts where the coronavirus test positivity rate in the community is under 5 % . 
" What we want is the whole country to be able to go back to school , and all of those case counts down to what we call the ‘ green states . ’ " Birx also said the way Americans can help get children back to school is by doing " what the President has asked , " which includes wearing masks , socially distancing and avoiding gatherings where you ca not socially distance .
