The key Senate GOP negotiators on the economic stimulus bill to respond to coronavirus were summoned about an ago hour to the Strom Thurmond Room , near Senate Majority Leader Mitch Mc Connell ’s office to " get a full update " about the emerging agreement , according to Sen . 
Marco Rubio . 
An aide to another participant said the briefing was called by Mc Connell . 
It could not be immediately determined from aides if the White House negotiating team was in the room but they were spotted entering Mc Connell ’s office which leads to the Strom Thurmond Room . 
Asked if this meeting was called to close the deal , Rubio replied : " They have not told us that but I do not know why else we would be meeting .
