Senate Majority Leader Mitch Mc Connell said the Trump administration is doing a " good job " on the coronavirus response so far . 
When CNN 's Lauren Fox asked him if the President needed to be more forthcoming about the risk the country was facing , Mc Connell jumped in to say , " I think we all need to work together to get a solution to the problem on a bipartisan , bicameral basis . " 
He would not answer a question about how his meeting at the White House went . 
CNN spotted him leaving the White House grounds earlier today .
