The White House approved a request by Washington Gov . 
Jay Inslee for a federal disaster declaration Sunday , Inslee said in a tweet Sunday . 
Inslee asked for the declaration in a letter to President Trump Friday to unlock a host of additional federal assistance programs for Washington residents affected by coronavirus , according to a statement from Inslee 's office . 
The declaration grants the state ’s request for assistance with crisis counseling and training to address the impact of the outbreak on the mental health of affected Washingtonians , Inslee 's statement read . 
Other disaster assistance programs requested in Inslee ’s letter remain under review by the White House , the statement read , including expanded unemployment assistance and basic food benefits .
