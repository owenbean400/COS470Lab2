Dr . Deborah Birx , coordinator of the White House coronavirus task force , told CBS ’ Margaret Brennan that mask mandates are critical for keeping schools open . 
" Are you asking governors to close the bars to keep schools open , is that your advice ? " 
Brennan asked Birx on " Face the Nation " Sunday . 
Birx also said that the US should close any space where people cannot wear masks . 
" The first thing you should do is close spaces where people cannot wear masks and we know where that is , " she said . 
" That 's bars and indoor restaurants – reduce capacity if needed , you may have to even close them . "
