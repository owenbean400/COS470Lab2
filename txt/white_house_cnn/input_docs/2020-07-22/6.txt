President Trump said he would make a decision over the next day on whether to mandate masks on federal property . 
While DC Mayor Muriel Bowser issued a mandate on masks on Wednesday , it wo not extend to federal buildings . 
Trump repeated his support of mask wearing , and again pulled out his mask to demonstrate his willingness to wear it ( though did not actually put it on ) . 
While Trump once mandated masks be worn by staff on the White House complex , the order expired and they are no longer mandatory for aides .
