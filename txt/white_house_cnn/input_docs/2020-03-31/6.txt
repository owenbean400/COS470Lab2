The White House told the US state of Illinois that it would receive 300,000 N95 masks -- but instead , the state got surgical masks , says Illinois Gov . 
J.B . Pritzker . 
This was the third federal shipment of PPE Illinois has received and it arrived Sunday . 
But the governor said Illinois had so far received only a " small fraction " of what the state had asked for . 
The state of Illinois currently has 5,057 confirmed coronavirus cases , including 73 deaths . 
Pritzker also called on President Donald Trump to use the Defense Production Act to direct companies to increase PPE production .
