The World Health Organization ( WHO ) has reported the greatest single day increase in global cases yet seen , with the total rising by 284,196 in 24 hours . 
The biggest increases were from the United States , Brazil , India and South Africa , according to a daily report . 
Deaths rose by 9,753 , the biggest one day increase since a record high of 9,797 deaths on 30 April . 
Previously , the greatest increase recorded by the WHO was 259,848 on 18 July . 
Deaths have been averaging 5,000 a day in July , up from an average of 4,600 a day in June .
