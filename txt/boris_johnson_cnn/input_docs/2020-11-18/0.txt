The head of the UK government 's ' Test and Trace ' program has been told to self isolate by the app that she is responsible for . 
Harding will be in self isolation until 26th November . 
She follows British Prime Minister Boris Johnson who is also self isolating after getting the same message . 
People in England and Wales were encouraged to download the NHS COVID 19 app when it was launched . 
There were teething issues with the app , which included it failing to alert people to their need to self isolate .
