UK Prime Minister Boris Johnson took to Twitter on Tuesday to thank citizens , scientists and health workers , shortly after the first Briton received a coronavirus vaccine . 
The UK is the first Western nation to begin vaccinating its citizens against Covid 19 , less than a week after approving the Pfizer/Bio NTech vaccine . 
" Today the first vaccinations in the UK against COVID 19 begin , " Johnson wrote . 
" Thank you to our NHS , to all of the scientists who worked so hard to develop this vaccine , to all the volunteers - and to everyone who has been following the rules to protect others . 
We will beat this together . "
