Health officials in British Columbia have confirmed Canada ’s first death in connection with coronavirus . 
The victim is a man in his 80s who had underlying health issues . 
He is one of two residents infected in a residential care home in North Vancouver . 
" This is obviously a very sad day for all of us , but especially for the family and loved ones of the man who passed away , " Adrian Dix , British Columbia ’s health minister , said in a statement . 
British Columbia 's health ministry announced six new cases of coronavirus on Saturday , two of which were residents of the long term care home . 
The other patient is a woman in her 70s and she remains in stable condition .
