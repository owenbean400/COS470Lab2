David Beasley , head of the United Nations ' World Food Programme ( WFP ) , has Covid 19 , according to a statement released on Thursday . 
Beasley said in a written statement that he is now working with his WFP team to trace back anyone he may have been in close contact with during the time he was unaware of the infection . 
Beasley aded : " I began feeling unwell this past weekend after returning to my home in the United States from an official visit to Canada and I took an early decision to go into self quarantine , five days ago . 
So far , my symptoms have been relatively light , and I am in good spirits . 
I am lucky to be close to my family and I have access to excellent medical support . "
