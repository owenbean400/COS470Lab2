The World Health Organization has reported a record increase in global coronavirus cases , rising by 183,020 in 24 hours , according to Reuters . 
The biggest increase was from North and South America with over 116,000 new cases , according to a daily report . 
Total global cases have passed 8.7 million with more than 461,000 deaths , according to the WHO . 
The previous record for new cases was 181,232 on 18 June .
